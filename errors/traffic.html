This Site Exceeded Its Traffic Limit


Show Caves of the World is a free service about show caves and show mines all over the world. It is a private site and has neither a sponsor nor other income. In other words: the server space is financed privately by the site owner.

This site is hosted with 3GB traffic per month, which was sufficient for several years now. But the number of visitors increases continually. Unfortunately, some search engines and other crawlers started to download enormous amounts of data, some download the complete site multiple times. And people started to link images from this site to include them into their own pages.

For every bit above 3GB there will be rather high additional costs. I am not willing to pay more than I am already paying, and so this site will go offline every time the free traffic is exceeded. It will be reopened next month.
