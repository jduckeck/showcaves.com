Republic of Botswana

Botswana


Botswana is a large country in the southern part of Africa. It is located just north of South Africa. The Capital is Gaborone, other main cities are Francistown, Lobatse, and Selebi-Phikwe. Botswana has an area of 581,730km² and a population of 1,392,000 people. The spoken languages are English and Setswana.
