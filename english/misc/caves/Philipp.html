History

 
1950 visited by the famous French archaeologist Abbe Breuil.
01-FEB-1951 proclaimed a National Monument (Namibia National Heritage Plaque).

Description

Philipp's Cave is a cave shelter or abri in insoluble granite rocks, formed by erosion. It is famous for its Bushman paintings which include hunting scenes, antelopes, and a White Elephant. The cave is located on the Ameib Game Ranch, in the Erongo Mountains.
