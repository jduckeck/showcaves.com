    Description

    The Giligili Cave is reached after a 10min walk following a creek through lovely bush. It is a river cave and involves wading through chest deep water and climbing.

    This cave is not developed and requires climbing. It is part of an ecotourism trip of Milne Bay Tourism. The half day trip takes three hours and includes an escorted vehicle ride, trekking and the spelunking tour. High physical fitness, Wellingtons, and old clothes are recommended. Bring clothes to change after the tour.
