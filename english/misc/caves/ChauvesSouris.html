    1967 visited by Dr V Aellen, M Frainier and Dr P Strinati.

    Description

    As the name suggests, these caves are full of bats. This is a natural cave higher up the mountain than the Roman Caves

  • El-Haouaria
    • which are antique quarries. A descent of 5m leads to a room 50 by 60m. The cave is designated as a Nature Reserve.
