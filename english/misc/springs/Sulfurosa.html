Description

This is a karst spring with the typical turquoise water caused by the high amount of dissolved limestone. It emerges from a underwater cave at the foot of a limestone cliff. The surrounding area is a public picknick area with wooden benches and desks. It is part of the Parque Nacional Tingo María.
