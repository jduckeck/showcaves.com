Description

天籁.蝴蝶泉 (Heavenly Music. Butterfly Spring) is a showcave with a sort of butterfly theme park. Even from far the huge metal Monarch Butterfly is visible, which was placed on the limestone wall above the cave entrance. And it is surrounded by a 36,000 m² park with butterfly waterfall, butterfly bridge, butterfly spring, and butterfly lake. It also has a butterfly garden with more than 200 different species. The name is derived from the butterfly-like form of the stalactites.
