Description

鱼龙洞 (Yú lóngdòng, Yulong Dong, Fish Dragon Cave) is a river cave which is visited partly by boat. So the ticket includes the guide and the boat ride. Several pages tell that the cave is currently closed, but we were not able to verify this.

The cave is located south of Nanyang town, just follow the road along the river.
