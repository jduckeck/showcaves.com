History

1980 opened to the public.

Description

Lingqui Dong (Lingqui Caves) are actually three caves, named Lingquan Cave, Qingfeng Cave and Aiyun Cave. Lingquan Cave is a river cave offering a 300m long boat ride. Qingfeng Cave is noted for it wind. With humid and warm outside temperatures the 14°C cool air is generally considered pleasant.
