History

JUL-2018 show cave opened to the public.

Description

奉節神仙洞 (Fengjie Shenxian Dong, Fengjie Cave of Immortals) is a rather new show cave, it was opened to the public in 2018. They are proud that the cave has 20,000 m² and 30,000 well-preserved stalactites which are three billion years old. We have no idea if each one is three billion years old, or the age of all 30,000 added gives 3 billion years. And the cave is connected to the cave and the road leads to the road. Concerning nonsense told about Chinese show caves, this is more or less average. It is extended by weird names for the formations, which we refuse to repeat.
