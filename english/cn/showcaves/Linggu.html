Description

Linggu Dong (Cave of the Valley of the Gods) is the third largest cave of Yixing. The tour crosses five chambers full of speleothems, stalagmites, stalactites, pilars, and socalled stone flowers. It is visited for centuries and is famous for it graffitties, poems engraved during the Song, Ming, and Qing dynasties.
