Description

灵岩洞 (Lingyan Cave, Lingam Cave) is also called 涵虚洞 (Hanxu Cave). It is located at an artificial lake and is a river cave, the resurgence of a cave river. The cave is visited by boat, the boat ride starts in front of the cave portal at a jetty. The most impressive formations in the cave have the name 仙人田 (Fairy Field).
