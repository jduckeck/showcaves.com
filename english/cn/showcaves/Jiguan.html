History

18th cty cave discovered.
APR-1993 opened to the public.
09-JAN-2012 awarded the title of National 5A Tourist Scenic Spot by China National Tourism Administration.

Description

Jiguandong was named after mount Jiguan (1,021m asl) where it is located. It is also known as Cockscomb Limestone Cave. The cave was discovered during the reign of Emperor Qianlong (1736-1796) in the Qing Dynasty (1644-1911).
