History


Description

新密神仙洞 (Xinmi Shenxian Dong, Xinmi Immortal Cave) is located in 神仙洞景区 (Shenxiandong Scenic Area), southwest of Zhengzhou. This is a provincial-level scenic attraction in Fuxi Mountain. The cave wqas formerly known as Kongshan Cave as well as Xianyu Lingyuan. The site is only of local fame, and thus it is not published in foreign languages. The cave is a karst cave with dripstones, too weird-coloured light to be enjoyable, with no specialties and rather small. It is said to be the largest show cave in the Central Plains area, which is obviously because it is the only show cave in the area.
