History

1976 investigated by a British Speleological Expedition to the Himalaya.

Description

Mahendra Gupha is a huge cave with many bats. It is developed as a tourist attraction, and many web pages tell about this cave. However, the whole situation does not make it a show cave in the western meaning of the word. It is necessary to walk for two hours to reach the cave, it has no light and no paths. Local tracking agencies make tours to and into the cave and it is a good idea to take one of that tours.
