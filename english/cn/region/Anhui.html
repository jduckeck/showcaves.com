Anhui Province


安徽 (Anhui) is located in east China, provincial capital and the largest city is Hefei, main rivers are Yangtze and Huai. The north is part of the North China Plain and the Huai River plain, densely populated but no caves. But there is a coal basin with numerous coal mines. The southwestern part os covered by the Dabie Mountains, the southeastern part by a series of hills and ranges. Here is some limestone which is karstified and some show caves.
