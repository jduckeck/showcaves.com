History

1958 Second Taiwan Strait Crisis with the People's Liberation Army.
1966 tunnel completed.
07-NOV-1992 abolition of Battle Field Administration.

Description

翟山坑道 (Zhaishan Tunnel) is also sometimes called Zhalshan Tunnel. It is one of numerous military underground structures which were abandoned after the abolition of the Battle Field Administration in 1992 and is now used as a tourist site.
