Description

The Silver Stream Cave is a place of great beauty. A steep cliff face with a waterfall has a cave behind the waterfall, which was used to build a temple inside. Hundreds of steps lead uphill to the temple. The place has a rather phantastic atmosphere, resembling Rivendell, the home of the Elves from the books Lord of The Ring.
