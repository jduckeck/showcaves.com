History

2001 declared a provincial-level cultural relics protection unit.

Description

水帘洞 (Shuiliandong) is a series of cave temples created from the Northern Zhou Dynasty to the Qing Dynasty. They are located in the Luban Gorge 25 kilometers northeast of Wushan County.
