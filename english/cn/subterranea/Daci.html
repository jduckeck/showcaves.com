Description

Daci Yan (Daci Rock) is a rocky hill with a cave temple right below the summit of Daci Mountain. The wooden buildings of the temple are built into the overhanging cliff, connected by bridges along the cliff. The 147 meters high standing Buddha is the largest sculpture of a standing Buddha in the country. The tempel is reached from the village, 580 m below, with a cable car. The spot is a popular tourist destination. The rocks consist of conglomerate, the huge abri is formed by erosion.
