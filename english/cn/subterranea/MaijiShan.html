History


Description

(Maijishan Grottoes, Maiji Shan Caves) is said to be one of the four famous cave temples of China, beneath Mogao, Yungang, and Longmen. From the entrance and ticket office its a 30 minutes uphill walk to the caves, but there is the possibility to take an electric vehicle for a small fee. At the second gate you can enter with the ticket for a self guided visit or pay extra for a private tour with extra grottos open for viewing.

Maijishan is a vertical cliff face with mostly artificial grottos which were cut into the vertical wall, creating sculpture. There are about 200 grottos and 7,000 sculptures. To reach them you have to clinb a steel staircase which was attached to the cliff face. Numerous caves are protected by metal gauze, but some are freely accessible.
