Archipelago of San Andrés, Providencia and Santa Catalina

Archipiélago de San Andrés, Providencia y Santa Catalina


The Archipelago of San Andrés, Providencia and Santa Catalina are several island which are located north of Colombia, just east of Nicaragua. It’s actually not part of South America, it is officially considered North America, or probably Middle America.
