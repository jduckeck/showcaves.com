Description

Sohoton Natural Bridge is a fantastic huge arch across Sohoton River. The portal is 7m high, 8m wide and 40m long. It is a remains of a former cave passage, walls and ceiling of the portal are full of speleothems. They were formed while they were still underground. Now they are partly covered by vegetation. The top of the bridge is forested.
