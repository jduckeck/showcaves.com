Callao Caves Tourist Zone


Callao Caves Tourist Zone is a caving and mountaineering paradise in Penablanca Town, Province of Cagayan. More than 300 caves arew known in this area, a few of them are used for spelunking adventures of various degrees of difficulty. There are archaeological, recreational and highly technical caves, both wet and dry. The caves are visited on day trips from Tuguegarao City.
