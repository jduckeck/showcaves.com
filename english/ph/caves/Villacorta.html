Description

Villacorta Cave is an undeveloped cave near the Brgy. Villacorta. It has a cave river with impressive rimstone pools and many other fine formations. Exceptional are the gypsum flowers. This cave is close to the more famous Cacupangan Cave but less accessible and more dificult to visit. Be careful and take appropriate caving gear with you. This is a completely undeveloped cave and the fact that it is visited by many people does not make it less dangerous.
