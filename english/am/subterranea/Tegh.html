Description

Տեղ (Cave Dwellings of Tegh) are the dozens of caves which were dug into a layer of porous rock which was easy to to excavate. The cave houses are today used as cellars and cow-sheds. Numerous huge caves are visible from highway M12 to the east.

However, even with the enormous amount of cave houses the site is actually not a tourist site, and we were not really convinced if we should list it. Also, there is no location and no development with trails, the caves are simply all around teh village and even in the surrounding villages. And another drawback is that this village is only a few km from the border to Azerbaijan, which adds the danger of border problems. The town is currently occupied by Azerbaijan [2022].
