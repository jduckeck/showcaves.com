History

1998 new entrance building with generator and toilets built.
01-DEC-1998 opened as a show cave.
2006 entrance building enlarged.

Description

Gruta do Natal (Christmas Cave) is located in the middle of Terceira Island. This lava tube is used by the locals every year at Christmas to celebrate a Mass on 25-DEC at 11. This tradition was started by Os Montanheiros in 1969, and is the reason for the name of the cave.

Originally an undeveloped cave, the number of visitors increased drastically during the 1990s, which caused some destruction. Originally there was a wooden building on top of the cave entrance, but the wood deceased and there was vandalism. So it was developed by the mountaineering club Os Montanheiros with a new stone building, the generator for the electric light, and toilets. The new building closes the entrance protecting the cave from vandalism. There is a staircase from the entrance building down into the cave. The cave is almost level and visitors walk on the natural cave floor.
