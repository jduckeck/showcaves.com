History

 
01-DEC-1998 opened as a show cave.
2003 entrance building enlarged.

Description

This lava tube is used by the locals every year at Christmas to celebrate a Mass (25-DEC, 11). Originally an undeveloped cave, the numer of visitor increased drastically during the last years, and so it was developd by the mountaineering club Os Montanheiros.
