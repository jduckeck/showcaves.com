History

1947 discovered.
1971 opened to the public.

Description

This is Portugal's largest caves system and the underground tour leads down through a series of colourfully lit caverns with some impressive speleothems. The last cavern, 110m below the surface contains a huge lake with artificial waterfalls and dramatic artificial fountains.

Exit is via a fast elevator to the surface.
