Distrito de Bragança

Bragança District



  • Grutas de Santo Adrião

The Distrito de Bragança (Bragança District) is the northeastern corner of Portugal. It is named after the district capital, the city of Bragança. The district covers most of the former province Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro.
