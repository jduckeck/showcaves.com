The country is divided into seven regions, which are subdivided into 18 districts in mainland Portugal Actually some districts belong to more than one region, so they do not share borders. There are also two autonomous regions, which are not part of mainland Portugal but the archipelagos Açores and Madeira. We decided to list using districts and autonomous regions.
