History

1927 Aliança Vinhos de Portugal founded by 11 associates led by Domingos Silva and Ângelo Neves, in Sangalhos.
1998 José Berardo becomes main shareholder Bacalhôa Vinhos de Portugal, S.A..
2007 Bacalhôa becomes the largest shareholder in the Aliança Vinhos de Portugal.

Description

The Aliança Underground Museum is located inside the wine cellar of the huge winery Aliança Vinhos de Portugal. It offers both, a classical wine cellar tour, and also a famous art museum. The art museum was founded by the famous art collector José Berardo, who used spare parts of the Aliança wine cellars for the exhibition of his personal art collection. The museum has eight different exhibitions: art, archaeology, ethnography, mineralogy, palaeontology, tiles and ceramics. One topic is the London Underground, the name is often shown with the London Underground symbol instead of the word "Underground" in the middle. And the map of museum is designed like the underground map too. Other impressive exhibits are the wide range of azulejos, hand-painted tiles, and the selection of ceramic animals by the ceramics company Bordallo Pinheiro.

And there are cellars full of huge oak barrels with maturing wines and brandies. Sometimes the exhibition rooms are full of wooden barrels. The production scale is quite impressive: the biggest oak barrels contain 17,000 litres of brandy each! They are self-made by the winery. At the end of the tour any adult participant gets a glass of sparkling wine for free.
