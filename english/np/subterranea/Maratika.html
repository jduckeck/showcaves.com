History


Description

Maratika Gufa (Maratika Cave) is part of Maratika Monastery, located only 185 km south west of Mount Everest. It is also known under the name Haleshi Mahadeva. It was according to legend the retreat of Mahadeva while he was hiding from the monster Bhasmasur. For both Hindus and Buddhist it is a famous pilgrimage centre.
