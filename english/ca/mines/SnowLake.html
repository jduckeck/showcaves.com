Description

The Snow Lake Mining Town Museum is located at the small mining town Snow Lake. It displays local mining history from the 1920s to present. Exhibit include a replica of a raise and a drift, mining machinery from drills to mucking machines, and miners clothes. There is no underground tour but at least some very good replicas. Currently one mine at Snow Lake is producing copper and zinc.
