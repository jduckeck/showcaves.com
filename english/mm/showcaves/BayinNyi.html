Description

This is a monastery at the foot of a karst tower, with numerous building including one which resembles a golden ship. There is a huge artificial lake in the middle of the complex and a thermal spring. Bathing in the spring is allowed, but there are separate baths for men and women. A steep staircase leads up to the cave. Like all other cave temples it is full of buddhas, pagodas, reliefs and stupas.
