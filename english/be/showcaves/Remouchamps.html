History

1912 opened to the public.
1924 electric light.
2010 LED lighting system installed in the Cathedral Hall.

Description

The first part of this cave is visited by foot, but the second part is visited by boat. The boat glides for almost 600 m over a winding subterranean river called Rubicon.

The dry part of the cave was inhabited by Paleolithic hunters some 8,000 years ago. The biggest hall in the cave is called Cathedral.
