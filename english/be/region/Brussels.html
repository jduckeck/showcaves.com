Brussels

Brussels-Capital Region


Any city on earth has underground structures. It's actually impossible to have a city without water supply, sewers, cellars and military structures. Not in all cities they are actually open to the public. Here they have sewers, underground pubs, historic cellars or casemats below a castle and even an underground diving site, which is quite unique.
