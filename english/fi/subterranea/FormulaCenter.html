History

1995 cart center founded.

Description

The Formula Center, sometimes also called Formula Center Helsinki, is an underground karting center. As far as we understand there is actually no fee for visiting the site, the fee is for driving the cart. That’s why we classified it as "factory", it is an underground venue. The site is a part of the bunker system of Helsinki.
