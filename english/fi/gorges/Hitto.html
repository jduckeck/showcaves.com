Description

Hitonhauta (Devil’s Tomb) is the largest gorge in Middle-Finland, 30 m to 40 m wide and up to 25 m deep. It contains a beautiful small pond, a waterfall and many caves. It was named after a legend, which tells that a spirit called Hitto (Devil) lived in this area.
