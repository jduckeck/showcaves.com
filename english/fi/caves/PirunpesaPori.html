Description

Pirunpesä (Devil’s Nest) is an extremely popular name in Finland, and so there are dozens. Most of them are rock formations or some kind of caves. This one is no exception it is a cave located in Lyttylä and is the third longest of Finland's well-known caves after Toskaljärvi cave and Kolin Repokallio cave .
