Longest Caves of Finland


Finland is dominated by a crystalline craton, and while it has several underground sites, most are mines or subterranea. Nevertheless, there are also numerous caves, but as they are erosional and tectonic caves, due to the lack of limestone areas, the caves a quite small. Bob Gulden's list of longest caves lists only caves longer than 10 km. Not a single cave from Finland is listed. So we decided to fill this gap and list a few caves of Finland. We are not officially managing this list, but obviously the interest to maintain such a list is not shared by the Finish cavers, but we do our best.
