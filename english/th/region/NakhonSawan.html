Nakhon Sawan Province


Nakhon Sawan Province is located at the center of Thailand, north of Bangkok. Near the city of Nakhon Sawan two rivers named Ping and Yom merge to form the Chao Phraya River. Nakhon Sawan is the capital of the privince and has its own amphoe. The name translates to Heavenly City.
