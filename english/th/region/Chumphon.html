Chumphon Province


Chumphon Province is the door to the south, where the country narrows, and the climate becomes hotter. There are plantations for gum tree, coffee and fruits. The climate becomes hotter, and the province is very much influenced by the monsoon with the heaviest rain falls during cotober and November. The Capital, which is also named Chumphon, is 463km from Bangkok.
