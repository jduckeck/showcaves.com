Description

ถ้ำแก้ว (Tham Kaew, Crystal Cave) is a single chamber with a wealth of speleothems. To reach it a hike and a little climbing uphill is required. The cave requires basic physical fitness, also good walking shoes and a lamp. It is strongly recommended to visit the cave in a group with the national park guide.

The cave is a through-cave, and it takes about half an hour from one entrance to the other.
