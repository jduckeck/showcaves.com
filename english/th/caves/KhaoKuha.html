Description

วัดถ้ำเขาคูหา (Wat Tham Khao Kuha) is a buddhist temple inside a cave, which is named ถ้ำคูหา (Tham Khuha, Khua Cave). The temple is also called ภูเขาคูหา (Phu Khao Kuha). The cave has rows of small and medium-sized buddha statues, in total about 50, and there is a huge reclining buddha statue. The main chamber is 17 m long and 8 m wide. This small temple is definitely off the beaten track, there are several pages in Thai including a Wikipedia page, but not a single one in English.
