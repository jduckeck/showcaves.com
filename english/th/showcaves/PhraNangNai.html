Description

(Tham Phra Nang Nai, Diamond Cave) is located a 10 minute walk from Railay. It is a rather small show cave with fine speleothems like stalagmites and rimstone pools. The cave is developed with an elevated trail made of wood and electric lamps mounted on the railings. That's definitely not very sophisticated and not good for the cave. Most visitors find the cave too expensive for the size.
