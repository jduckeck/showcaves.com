Description

ถ้ำเมืองออน (Tham Muang On) is located in a karst tower off the road to Sankamphaeng. In front of the cave is Wat Tham Muang On, a temple, and the cave is also used as a Buddhist temple. A long staircase with dragon sculptures as railings on both sides leads to the rather small cave entrance Inside the cave is a huge symmetric stalagmite which is quite spectacular. It is considered a lingam. The cave contains several Buddha statues, but is otherwise unaltered.
