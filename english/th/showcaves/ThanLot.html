History

 
12-FEB-1980 area declared a National Park.

Description

Tham Than Lot National Park has two caves, Than Lot Noi Cave and Than Lot Yai Cave. The caves are accessible through a 2.5-kilometer nature trail along the stream. One of the caves even has the worlds tallest stalagmite. One of them, at least...

A portion of the tour is done by bamboo raft. There is also a possibility to visit a bat cave as third cave, but most tourists skip that. The floor is covered with 5cm bat guano and the smell is overwhelming.

The best time of the year to visit the caves is from February to April. While it is possible to visit the trail self guided, it is recommended to take a park ranger as a guide. Tham Than Lot National Park is also called Chaloem Rattanakosin National Park. It has an area of 54km² with pristine forest, waterfalls, and numerous caves.
