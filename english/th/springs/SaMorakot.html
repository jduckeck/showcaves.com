Description

Sa Morakot or Emerald Pool is a karst spring in the middle of the rain forest. It is surrounded by tufa rims and the typical blue water is a result of the high content on dissolved limestone. As typical for Thai springs and waterfalls it is a popular bathing destination for the locals. Nearby Blue Pool is not for bathing, the water comes from a hot spring and bathing would be dangerous.
