Description

Tham Pha Thai (Pha Thai Cave) is a cave temple in a natural cave. Only 700m from the Highway 1 is the large parking lot. From here 283 steps lead down to the floor of the cave. There is a principal Buddha image in the cave entrance. Further on there are some stalactites and stalagmites.
