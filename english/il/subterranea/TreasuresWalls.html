History

2001 inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Description

The name of the אוצרות בחומה is translated Treasures in the Walls Museum or Treasuries in The Wall. The entrance is located in the ditch between outer and inner city walls and best reached on a short walk from Chaim Weizmann road. It is more or less a local history museum which we would normally not list, but it is located in the catacombs of the city wall, which is the reason why we listed it.
