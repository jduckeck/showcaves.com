History

864 lava tube formed during a volcanic eruption of Mt. Fuji by the Aokigahara Maruo lava stream.
1929 designated a Natural Monument of Japan.

Description

The 西湖コウモリ穴 (Saiko Komori-ana) is named after Saiko (Lake Sai) nearby. Together with Ryugu Doketsu Iriguchi and Narusawa Fuketsu it is located in the Aokigahara lava flow of the Fuji volcano. The Saiko Bat-Cave Information Center organizes guided walks through the nearby suicide forest and the cave. Unlike the other caves in the area it is not a cold trap, contains no ice and is populated by bats.
