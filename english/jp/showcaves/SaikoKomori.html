History


Description

The Saiko Komori-ana (西湖コウモリ穴) is named after Saiko (Lake Sai) nearby. Together with Ryugu Doketsu Iriguchi and Narusawa Fuketsu it is located in the in the Aokigahara lava flow of the Fuji volcano. The Saiko Bat-Cave Information Center organizes guided walks through the nearby suicide forest and the cave. Unlike the other caves in the area it is not a cold trap, contains no ice and is populated by bats.
