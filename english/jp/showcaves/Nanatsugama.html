History

AUG-1954 cave discovered and first explored by the locals including the headmaster of Nanakama Jinjo Elementary School.
JUL-1964 designated a scenic spot by the Governor of Nagasaki.
DEC-1983 designated a natural monument by the Ministry of Education.

Description

The 七ツ釜鍾乳洞 (Nanatsugama shōnyūdō) or Shimizu Cave is formed inside a 30Ma old limestone. The small karst area has so far 35 known caves.

The cave also offers cave trekking tours for groups after appointment.

The cave visit is completed by a small museum which shows rocks and fossils from the Paleozoic to Cenozoic. Most of the exhibits are fossils from the local limestone or speleothems from the cave.
