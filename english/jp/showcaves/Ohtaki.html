Description

大滝鍾乳洞 (Otaki limestone cave) is a large cave, listed as the 26th deepest of Japan. It is the most popular show cave in Gujo Prefecture. Its outstanding sight is an underground waterfall, which is 30m high and thus the highest in Japan. The biggest chamber on the tour with numerous spelothems is named 天上界 (Celestial World).

The cave entrance is high up at the hill, and it is reached from the ticket office by a funicular, a cable car on rails. Quite exceptional is the cabin which is built on wood.
