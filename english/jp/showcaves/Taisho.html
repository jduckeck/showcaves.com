History

15-JAN-1921 cave discovered.

Description

大正洞 (Taishōdō) is a dripstone cave with various speleothems. The cave is also called Ushi-kakushi (Cow Hiding Cave), as the locals were hiding their cows inside the cave during wartime, so they could not be stolen.
