Nippara shonyudo

Nippara Limestone Cave


Useful Information

Location: Nippara, Okutama-machi, Tokyo.
Bus from Okutama Station on the JR Ome Line. Weekday: 5-minute walk from Nippara Shonyudo bus stop. Weekend and holiday: 20-minute walk from Higashi Nippara bus stop.
Open: APR to NOV daily 8-17.
DEC to MAR daily 8:30-16:30.

Fee: Adults Yen 600, High school students Yen 400, Elementary school students Yen 300. [2012]
Classification: Karst cave
Light: electric.

History


Description

Nippara shonyudo is a sacred site since the early Edo period. The cave is one of the biggest in the Kanto region and famous for its beauty.
