Description

日原鍾乳洞 (Nippara shonyudo, Nippara Limestone Cave) is a sacred site since the early Edo period. The cave is one of the biggest in the Kanto region and famous for its beauty. The cave entrance is the resurgence of a cave river, the entrance for the visitors is located right above in a fossil level of the cave. The most impressive part of the cave is the main chamber which is really spacious. Unfortunately it is equipped with coloured LED light.

Nippara Cave is also written Hihara Cave or Hibara Cave, obviously there are completely different transliterations. We are at a loss how this name is actually pronounced.
