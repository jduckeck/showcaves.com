Description

Ryugashi Cave is the biggest cave of in Tokai District. It is named after Mount Ryugashi, where it is located inside. The cave entrance is at the foot of the mountain, the cave is explored on self guided tours. The cave ends in a small speleologic msueum. The cave has some nice speleothems and a 30m high waterfall.
