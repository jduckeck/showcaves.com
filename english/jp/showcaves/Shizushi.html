Description

質志鐘乳洞 (Shizushi Cave) is the only limestone cave in Kyoto Prefecture. The cave is a vertical cave 25m deep, which is developed by steep iron stairs. The stairs and ladders are actually more interesting than the cave, the steeper parts are enclosed in a sort of cage of stainless steel bars.
