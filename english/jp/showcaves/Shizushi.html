History

JAN-2023 heavy snowfall damaged the facilities in the park and park closed until MAY.

Description

質志鐘乳洞 (Shizushi shonyudo, Shizushi Stalactite Cave) or 質志洞 (Shizushi do) is the only limestone cave in Kyoto Prefecture. The cave is a vertical cave, 25 m deep, which is developed by steep iron stairs and ladders. The stairs and ladders are actually more interesting than the cave, the steeper parts are enclosed in a sort of cage of stainless steel bars.
