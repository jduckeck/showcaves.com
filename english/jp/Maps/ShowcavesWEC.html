WEC Show Caves in Japan


This map of tourist caves was provided by the now gone Whole Earth Club (WEC). The creator of the map was Sumio Kondo and it was embedded into a page which listed the cave names to the numbers. When they dissolved in 2001 the pages were hosted by the Japan Speleo Page (JSP). The WEC were merged into the Speleological Society of Japan (SSJ). As far as we know they do not have this map anymore, but you can check out the waybackmachine links below.
