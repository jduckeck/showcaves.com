Yamagata Prefecture - Yamagata-ken


Yamagata Prefecture is located in the Tōhoku region of Honshu island, in the northern third on the coast towards China. Most of the prefecture is mountainous and its borders with neighboring prefectures are formed by various mountain ranges.
