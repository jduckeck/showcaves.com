Okinawa


Okinawa is the prefecture with the most caves in Japan. Most of it is composed of reef limestone which is karstified. There are about 125 known caves in the area with 16 caves open to the public.

The Show Caves of This Prefecture:

  • Gyokusen-do
  • Futenma Gongen
  • Ishigaki Do
  • Kin-nisshu-do
  • Gold Cave Café and Bonsai Garden
  • Hirano-do
  • Hoshino-no ana
  • Ryugujo shonyudo
