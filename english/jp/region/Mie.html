三重県

Mie Prefecture


三重県 (Mie Prefecture) is located on the southeastern coast of Honshu island, in the Kansai region. The capital is Tsu, the largest city is Yokkaichi. The island has a huge peninsula named Kii, the prefecture covers the eastern part of this peninsula. The prefecture has a fertile coastal plain and two mountain ridges. There is a spectacular gorge and a single show cave, which is rather small. The prefecture actually lacks underground sites.
