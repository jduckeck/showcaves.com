Kōchi-ken - Kōchi Prefecture


Kōchi Prefecture is located on the island of Shikoku, around the large bay at the southern coast. Most of the province is mountainous, but around Kōchi and Nakamura is there a coastal plain. The highest mountain is 1,506 m asl. It has only one show cave, Ryugado, but it is considered one of Japan's top three caves.
