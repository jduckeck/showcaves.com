Kagoshima Prefecture - Kagoshima-ken


Kagoshima Prefecture is the southern tip of Kyushu, the third-largest island of Japan plus the Ryukyu Islands. A chain of active and dormant volcanoes, including the great Sakurajima, which is one of Japan's most active volcanoes.
