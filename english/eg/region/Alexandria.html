Alexandria Governorate


Alexandria Governorate is the city of Alexandria, which was historically the capital of Egypt. Today it is the second-largest city in Egypt. Located on the Mediterranean Sea, it has one of the most important harbours in Egypt. It is industrial and contains 40% of Egyptian industries.
