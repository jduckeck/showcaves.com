History

07-DEC-2012 cave protected for its natural beauty with Alcaldia Resolution N°263-2012-MPH.

Description

Cueva de Almendras (Cueva De Almendras) is named after nearby town Almendras. The entrance passage is 1.80 m high and 1.50 m wide, but it widens to form the 45 m long and 11 m high chamber.
