History

 
1957founded by Eric and Jaymie.

Description

Lure has it, that The Show Cave was founded by Eric and Jaymie after returning from a trip to Egypt. It seems their hunt for ancient Babylonian electronics was as fruitless as the attemt to expand western consciousness with the Show Cave. Now the cave displays art created by contemporary artists.
