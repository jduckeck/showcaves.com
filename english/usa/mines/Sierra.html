History

~1900 silver discovered.
1960s exploration failed.
1982 Sierra Silver Mine Tour, Inc. founded and mine developed.

Description

Sierra Silver Mine Tour is located in the Coeur d’Alene district, a mining area with a 120-year history of mining. Sierra Mine’s orebodies were never rich enough to warrant active mining, so ownership of the mine changed several times. There were various attempts to mine silver, the most thorough exploration took place in the 1960s. The results were disappointing and so the mine was closed again. Later it was used to teach local high school students mining skills. As a result, the tour is not explaining the whereabouts of this special mine, but the history and mining techniques of the area.
