Description

Compromise Mine is located on Aspen Mountain, above the Little Nell Ski Run. This is a working mine, currently used on an exploratory basis. The tour is guided by experienced local miners, who will explain the differences between modern mining and historic mining. To reach the mine, guests are driven to the mine entrance with four wheel drive vehicles. Then they board an 18 gauge mine car and ride 700m underground.

This is actually where Aspen silver mining began. When silver seekers crossed the pass from Leadville, they found ore at this place, and soon realized they found the mother lode. Numerous small mines existed in this area, which were finally united, to end a continuous struggle. Hence the name Compromise Mine.
