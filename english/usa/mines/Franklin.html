02-JUN-1964 opened to the public.

Description

Franklin Mineral Museum & Mine Replica is located in the fluorescent mineral capital of the world. The museum explains zinc mining in the area dating back to 1848 and earlier. It has a life size mine replica which shows the visitor examples of mining methods used in the Franklin mines.
