Description

Twin Creek Crystal Mine is one of numerous fee mines where you can dig for quartz or rock crystals. This site has not published any peculiarities, so we cannot tell much about it. As far as we see it is comparable to the others. Wear sun protection, bring rain protection, boots or trekking shoes, bring tools for collected and boxes to store your finds. You can keep what you find.
