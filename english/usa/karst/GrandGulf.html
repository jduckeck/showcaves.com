History

1898 described by Luella Agnes Owen in her book Cave Regions of the Ozarks and Black Hills.
1971 designated a National Natural Landmark.
1984 Missouri State Park created.

Description

Grand Gulf is an exceptional and rare karst feature, a spectacular collapsed cave system. A narrow gorge stretches for nearly two kilometers with walls almost 40 m high, making the chasm deeper than it is wide. It is not entered, but there are trails along the rim and outlooks. Educational signs explain the origin and geology of the site.
