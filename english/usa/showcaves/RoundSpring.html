History

1932 opened to the public.
1972 incorporated into the Ozark National Scenic Riverways.

Description

Round Spring Cave is close to the famous spring with the same name, but not connected. It is said to be heavily decorated with formations. This cave is only guided with lanterns twice a day by the National Park Service.
