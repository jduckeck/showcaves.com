History

1880 discovered.
1929 opened for the public as Nameless Cave.

Description

It is said that Crystal Cave Park is one of America’s most beautiful caverns. Everyone has to decide this for himself, but a statistical fact is that crystals line 80% of the walls and ceiling of this cave. They are also called dogtooth spar.

The cave also has popcorn calcite and boxwork, and of course some stalactites and stalagmites.
