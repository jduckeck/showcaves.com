History


Description

The hamlet Natural Bridge in New York State was named after an actual "natural bridge" which is located in the middle of the small village. The Indian River, which runs through the village, goes underground through a cave for a short distance. The cave was a show cave some time ago, operated by David Morgan under the name Natural Bridge Caverns. The tour through Natural Bridge Caverns was an underground boat trip on the Indian River which was about 400 m long. The wooden hut, which was the ticket office and gift shop of the show cave
