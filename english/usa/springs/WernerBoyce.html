History

2001 park opened.

Description

Werner-Boyce Salt Springs is a series of karst springs along the western coast of Florida. The main spring is rather small, but almost 100m deep. It is a salt water spring but is unfortunately not accessible at the moment. An extension of the 800m trail which would include the spring is planned.
