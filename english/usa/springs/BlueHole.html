History


Description

Blue Hole Spring is pool with extremely blue water, a colour caused by the high amount of limestone in the water. The pool is a karst spring located at the Florida Caverns State Park. The water flows after some 700 m into the Carters Mill Branch, which is a tributary to the Chipola River.
