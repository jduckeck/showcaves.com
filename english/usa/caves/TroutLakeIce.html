Trout Lake Ice Cave


Useful Information

Location: 9 km southwest of Trout Lake, Gifford Pinchot National Forest. From White Salmon Washington Route 141 north to Trout Lake, then west on Forest Road 24 (Carson-Guler road) for 9 km. 2 km west of the Mt. Adams Ranger Station on the Carson-Guler road.
