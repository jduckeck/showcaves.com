History

1984 underground store built.

Description

Hollow Mountain is the name of an underground store, which is part of a gas station at Hanksville, Utah. It was created in 1984, using over 100 cases of dynamite, which is about 2.5 tons. The gas station is standard, but its store is completely underground inside the mountainside. We can only guess that they built it underground to save energy for air conditioning.
