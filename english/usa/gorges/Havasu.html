Description

The Havasu Falls are a series of waterfalls and rimstone pools, tufa deposits and growing waterfalls. The red rocks of Grand Canyon, the turquise of the water, and the bright green of the vegetation make this place a unique and magic location.

The falls are located only 2.4 kilometers from Supai, the main town of the Havasupai tribe, in the middle of the Havasupai tribal lands. Actually Havasu Falls is the name of a single 30m high waterfall.
