The southern Part of Arkansas belongs to the Gulf Coastal Plains. This lowlands have no caves. The highland in the northern Arkansas owes many caves, especially in the north west corner of the state. This area belongs to the Ozarks.

As most of the Ozarks belongs to Missouri, see the Missouri-page for more information about this region.
