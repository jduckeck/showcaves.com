Lai Chau Province


Lai Chau is a northwestern province of Vietnam at the border to China. It is densely covered with vegetation, forestry is the main economic resource. The climate is hot and humid, with an annual average temperature 23°C and an annual average rainfall of 2.5m. This least populated province of Vietnam, with 34 inhabitants per km².
