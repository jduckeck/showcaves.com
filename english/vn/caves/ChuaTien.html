History


Description

Chua Tien (Pagoda of Fairies) is a cave temple built in a small cave in an elephant-shaped karst tower. It is located south of Lang Son town, half a kilometre from Ky Lua bridge. It was built during the reign of Le Thanh Ton (*1460-✝1497).
