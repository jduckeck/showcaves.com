Description

Gua Wareh is a small cave located at the foot of the North Limestone Mountains, in Kedumulyo Village. The river cave is a resurgence which is one of the main sources of water for the local village for daily needs and to irrigate rice fields and fields. The spring is also used as public bathing area. The cave has a huge portal and a sort of concrete jetty. The cave behind is gated. The visit requires swimming or a boat.
