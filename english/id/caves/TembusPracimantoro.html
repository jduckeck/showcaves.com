Description

Goa Tembus Pracimantoro is a small horizontal cave which is located only 100 m from the Karst Museum Indonesia, at the road leading to the museum. It is actually the cave which is closest to the museum and has a bus station nearby. It has a trail and staircase leading to the entrance, an educational sign and is very easy to visit. It is a through-cave crossing the hill completely. The passage is high enough to walk upright and the floor is level. It is intended as a completion for a museum visit, for normal visitors as well as school classes.
