History

1928 first explored by Vladimir Horvat.
1928 opened to the public.
1978 revovated, new rails and electric light installed.

Description

Špilja Vrlovka is a cave with a small cave river and numerous dripstone formations. A specialty are the yellow formations.

The cave was open for a very long time, excavations brought remains of animals and humans which took shelter in the cave. Famous are finds of the Lasinje Culture around 2500 BC.
