Description

Vražji Prolaz (Devil's Gorge) is one of the most beautiful canyons in Croatia. The brook Jasle flows though the narrow gorge, surrounded by steep cliffs up to 100m high. Visitors follow an iron path, bridges buit right above the fast flowing brook. The narrow gorge receives almost no sun. The water has formed smooth erosional forms, which seem in contrast to the surrounding steep cliffs.
