Hrvatsko Zagorje


  • Krapina Cave

Hrvatsko Zagorje translates Croatian upland or hinterland, and this historic region comprises the whole area north of Medvednica mountain up to Slovenia in the north and west. Croatian people usually refer to this region simply as Zagorje, but there is another region which bears the same name in Slovenia. Hence it is generally called Hrvatsko Zagorje which means Croatian Zagorje.
