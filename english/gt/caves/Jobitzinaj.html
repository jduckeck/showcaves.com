Description

Cueva Jobitzinaj is another entrance of the Actún Kan cave system, on the opposite side of the hill. Unlike the Actún Kan part, this cave is not developed as a show cave. Jobitzinaj is more extensive. The access to the cave is not restricted, and it is a rather easy beginners cave. Nevertheless, we recommend getting a guide at the Actún Can ticket office.
