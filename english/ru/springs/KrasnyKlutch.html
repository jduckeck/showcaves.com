Description

Krasny Klutch (Red Spring) is the biggest Vaucluse type spring of fresh water in Europe. And it is one of the biggest karst springs in the world. The water in the spring is saturated with limestone, so every second the water brings more than one kilogram of limestone to the surface, which makes about 100 tons per day. The water fills in two lakes with greenish-bluish water in the valley below.
