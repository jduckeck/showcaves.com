Description

Пещера Нежная (Peshchera Nezhnaya) is the nice or gentle cave, which obviously means it is quite pleasant, with many speleothems. It is also rather small. It is visited on regular guided tours, but actually it is not a show cave as we know it from other countries. The trail is quite rough and mostly just clay and flowstone, there few concrete paths, which requires some surefootedness and good shoes. Nevertheless the tours are not cave trekking tours, no special caving knowledge or physical fitness required.
