Description

The gallery of photographer Otto Rogge from Stuttgart, Germany, is located underground, like most houses at White Cliffs. So if you want to see a dugout with phantastic photographs, but without opal display, this is the place to go. And as it is a shop and gallery, not a museum, it is free. It is open all year, only on bad weather days the road may be a sump and the gallery is not reachable. Fortunately this happens very seldom in the desert.
