Description

The White Cliffs Underground Motel is located at the small desert town White Cliffs, where most of the buildings were actually excavated into the soft red sandstone. The three star AAA rated hotel has a swimming pool, a restaurant and a bar. The rooms are signed as dugouts. The hotel is recommended for its pub with many local guests, and the good food.
