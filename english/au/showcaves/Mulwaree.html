History

1865 discovered by Charles Chalker, the first caretaker appointed by the Government.
1865 opened for public inspection.

Description

Mulwaree Cave is actually the upper level of the expansive Wollondilly Cave, It is said to be one of Wombeyan’s most spectacular caves. It is also a little strenuous, as there are 226 steps to negotiate along the only 200 m long trail, which is the reason why this short tour takes one hour. As it requires some fitness, only a small part of the visitors is interested in this tour. As a result the cave is open only on long weekends, when the caves have the highest visitor numbers. If you are with a group we recommend strongly to arrange a visit.
