History

1800s discovered.
1968 opened as a show cave.

Description

Carey's Cave is located in the Wee Jasper Valley, close to Canberra. Seven chambers are visited on the almost horizontal tour with only 30 steps. Rather exceptional is the changing temperature of the cave, which is between 15 and 17°C.
