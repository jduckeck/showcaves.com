History

1865 discovered by Charles Chalker, the first caretaker appointed by the Government.
1875 opened for public inspection.

Description

Kooringa Cave is the smallest and the most heavily decorated show cave at Wombeyan. It is only a single chamber with pristine white columns and contrasting iron-rich draperies.
