History

2000 reopened.

Description

Cathedral Cave features an enormous entrance chamber which gave the cave its name. Probably this was the reason why it was first used as a show cave. But this early visitations were not very good for the cave, the visitors did not know about protecting the cave. The cave was closed several decades ago, but now, after some restoration work, the cave is again worth a visit.
