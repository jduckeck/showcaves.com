History

1966 Peter Rowe first visited Alice Springs and decided to live in the Outback.
1967 Peter Rowe moved to Coober Pedy.
1982 Konnie and Peter Rowe converted their underground home into a studio and gallery.

Description

This pottery is completely underground, the studio where the pottery is made, the storage, the oven, and the gallery where visitors can see the finished work. Unfortunately it is gone, there is no successor and road signs are removed and the Google entry is gone. However, the pottery they produced still exists and is still sold on etsy and other platforms.
