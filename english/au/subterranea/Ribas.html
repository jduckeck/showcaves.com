Description

We never heard about something like this: it is an underground camping site. So bring your tents and camp under the earth. And quite comfortable we have to say: no wind, no rain, clean facilities, and entertainment room with tv and internet. There are also some budget rooms, if you are too lazy to pitch your tent. And if you can't sleep, there is a Nightly Mine Tour too.
