History

1982 bought by Doug and Gloria Saunders.
1983 underground mine opened to the public.
1985 bought by Val and Jim Deacon.
1998 bought by Ian, Ross and Pam Cameron.

Description

The Bobby Dazzler Mine Rubyvale is a fossicking site where visitors can collect local precious stones. It also offers underground mine tours into a gem mine. Rubyvale, as the name says is located in the Sapphire Gemfields. The village has gem cutters, jewellers, a pub, cafés, general store, service station and accommodation providers. But while there are numerous fossicking sites, this is one of only two underground mine tours in the area. Unfortunately they moved their website to facebook, so there is no actual information available.
