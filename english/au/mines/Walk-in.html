History

1880s first red on black opal discovered at Lightning Ridge.
1963 Walk in Mine opened to the public.
2017 renamed Opal Mine Adventure.

Description

The Walk-in Mine has underground tours which are followed by a film full of information about opal mining in the Ridge, shown in an underground theatre. There is also a mining display, sale of souvenirs and opals. The site was renamed Opal Mine Adventure in 2017, but it seems the name Walk in Mine is so famous that they now use both names. But they changed their domain name and the name of their facebook page. Hopefully they will refrain from moving completely to facebook.

Lightning Ridge is the place where the first red on black opal was discovered in the 1880s. This is, until today, the most rare and hence the most valuable opal in the World. But the opal fields in the vicinity of the Ridge are exhausted, mining takes place about an hour drive away
