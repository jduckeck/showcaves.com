History

2008 opened to the public.

Description

Rio Secreto is a cave which was discovered inadvertently by the owner of the land, Don Cleo, while chasing one of his animals. The cave is rather exceptional, as its one of very few caves which are not submerged. Most caves are filled with water, either sweet water or seawater, or both with a halocline. This cave is by chance located at the current level of the groundwater. This is really a coincidence, as it was formed long ago by groundwater at the same level. Then the cave became a fossil cave with many speleothems and was flooded by the rising sea levels after the end of the last ice age some 8,000 years ago.

Today it is possible to visit this cave by wading through mostly knee-deep water. There are some places where it is necessary to swim. Actually, this is a unique cave, somewhere between a show cave and a cave trekking tour. There are no electric lights and visitors are equipped with helmets with head lamps, and swimming suits. Of course, this visit is more demanding than a paved trail through a normal cave, so visitors must be at least four years old and not more than 120 kilograms.

Beneth the originaly wading tour the operators now offer all kinds of sportive actvities, This includes rapelling, biking and climbing. So all other tours except the "classic" tour are only for sportive and physically fit participants. All tours are offered with transport from Riviera Maya or from Cancún.
