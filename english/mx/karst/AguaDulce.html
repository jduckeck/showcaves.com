Description

Cenotes Agua Dulce (Freshwater Cenotes) seems to be the company which operates the Cenote Agua Dulce. Or probably it’s the name of the cenote. This cenote is of the type underground cave chamber with openings in the ceiling and speleothems. The natural light is not sufficient, so there is electric light.

There are actually two different cenotes only 350 m apart, and its quite unclear if they are operated by the same or different operators, at least there seems to be a combo ticket for visiting both. The other one is called Cenote Palomitas.
