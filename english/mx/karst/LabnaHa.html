Description

The operators at Cenote LabnaHa offer any possible activity in connection with cenotes and underwater caves. It starts with boats and snorkeling, visiting the above water level of the cave, swim and snorkel in the cenote, dive tours of the cave. But the really exceptional dives are all kinds of educations tours, so you can get the licence as a cave diver here, certidfied by the NSS. And its possible to book five hour cave dives if you own such a licence.
