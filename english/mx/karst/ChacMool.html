Description

Cenote Chac Mool (claw of the jaguar) is one of the most popular diving sites in the area. This cenote is rather well developed with bathrooms and a restaurant at the entrance. It is possible to dive the cave in both directions, upstream and downstream. The normal dive takes about 40 minutes, is rather easy and less than 15 m deep. It is suitable for beginners.
