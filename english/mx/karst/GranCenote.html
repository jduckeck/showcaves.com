Description

Gran Cenote has a placid, clear pool, and snorkelers can make their way through a massive, arched cavern and down along a tunnel. The cave system is called Sac Aktun (White Cave in Mayan) because of the white stalagmites. The place is one of the less developed, which means rather small, less crowded and cheaper than others.
