Description

Cenote San Ignacio is located in the village Chochola. It is of the closed type, a cavern which is electrically lit and has a music system. The ceiling is up to 8 m high and full of stalactites. The cenote is privately owned and well equipped with bathrooms, showers, and dressing rooms. There is even a children's play area and a restaurant. On the surface is a huge lawn, a swimming pool, huts with barbecue and more.
