Description

El Puente de Dios (God's Bridge) is a natural bridge through which the San Lucas River flows. The natural bridge is an actual bridge as it is crossed by the A Rayones road. A long staircase leads down into the brdge to a viewing platform. The bridge has a sort of gorge inside, with steep walls, and it is not possible to cross it.
