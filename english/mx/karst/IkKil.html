Description

Cenote Ik Kil is a perfectly round, well-type cenote with an diameter of 60m and a depth of 40m. It is an open shaft, the water level is 25m below the surface with its exuberant vegetation. A grand stairway leads visitors down into the water, which is ideal for swimming.
