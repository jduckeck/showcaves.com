Description

A few minutes from walk from the southern entrance of Deer Cave, is Lang's Cave, named after a local guide who showed it to the cave explorer's of the 1977/1978 expedition. This is a cave that should not be missed. It contains a wonderful array of speleothems, including some unusual helictites.
