History

1950 to 1960 used by the communists as a hideout.
early 1970's mining for tin at the Gergasi Chamber.
1998 opened as a show cave.

Description

Gua Tempurung is a large cave system with beautiful speleothems like stalagmites, stalactites, rim stone pools, curtains, straws, calcite crystals, and pillars. It is located inside Gunung Tempurung (Tempurung Mountain), which it is named after. The marble and limestone mountain is crossed completely by the 1.6 kilometre main passage with its cave river.

This cave is very well developed with paths, stairs and electric light. It is a really young show cave, as it was developed in 1998. Before this it was already toured a lot, because it is so huge, the visit had no big difficulties.

It is now possible to choose between four tours of different length, from a short show cave tour to a long cave trekking tour. The shortest tour is very easy and enters the cave at the main entrance. It leads into the Golden Flowstone Chamber, where it turns around at platform two. This tour is very easy with only a very small ascend.

A little longer is the Top of the World Tour which leads to the place called Top of the World, which is the highest point of the main passage. Here at platform five the developed part of the cave ends. While the tourist tour ends here and visitors return on the same path, there is also an adventure tour which returns through the river in the lower level. And finally there is a cave trekking tour which completely crosses the through cave along the river, which is close to the original cave visits which were made before the cave was developed.
