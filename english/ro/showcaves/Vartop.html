History

1974 speleological exploration by a team led by Professor Iosif Viehmann from the "Emil Racoviță" Speleological Institute in Cluj.
2010 opened to the public as a show cave.

Description

Peștera Ghețarul de la Vârtop (Vartop Glacier Cave) has numerous different names. It is known as Peștera Minunată (Wonderful Cave) or Ghețarul de la Casa de Piatră (Stone House Glacier).
