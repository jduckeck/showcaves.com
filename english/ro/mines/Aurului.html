Description

The Muzeul Aurului din Brad (Gold Museum) in the town Brad is said to have the largest collection of ores containing gold and silver in Europe. It also has the largest collection of semi-precious stones in the world. But the most spectacular exhibits are the 1,200 gold-bearing or gold-studded rocks of the exhibition. But there are almost 1,000 other interesting minerals, mining equipment, photographs, and documents about the local mining history. This area is called Muntii Metaliferi (Ore Mountains), and most of the gold minerals are from this so-called Polygon of Gold. The reason is the fact that the mines are located in a specific are which is very rich in metal ores, especially gold. The

The Brad Mineralogical Collection is owned by the national company "MINVEST" SA from Deva.
