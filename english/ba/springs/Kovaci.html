History


Description

Kovači or Kovači Jama is called a pothole or doline, but actually it is the resurgence of the Sujica river, which flows through Duvajisko polje. The catchment area is the Grabovačka planina bare karst with its hundreds of dolines. The spring is located in the hamlet Tubolja at the foot of the limestone hill which is about 200 m higher.
