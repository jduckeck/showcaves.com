History

1970 protected by the Resolution of the National institute for protection of monuments of culture and natural rarities B&H.

Description

Vaganska pecina is one of the most beautiful caves in the Republic of Srpska according concentration and variety of speleothems. The cave is habitat for several rare species of bats: Barbastella barbastellus, Myotis bechsteinii, Myotis emarginatus and Rhinolpus ferrumequinum.
