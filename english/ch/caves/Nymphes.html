1998 paths renovated.

Description

The Gorges mystérieuses de Tête-Noire (gorge of the black steps) is a wild and narrow gorge near Trient. A footpaths allows visitor to walk along the gorge, called sentier gorges mystérieuses. This trail is very educative, as several text panels with explanatory text tell background information about the place.

A special sight of the gorge is the Grotte aux Nymphes (Cave of Nymphs), a rather small cave formed by the wild river.
