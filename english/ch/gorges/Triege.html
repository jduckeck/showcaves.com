History

1870 trails constructed.
1988 spiral staircase constructed.

Description

The Gorges du Triège is the gorge of the torrent Triège, whichs springs in the val d'Emaney. It is a tributary of the Trient, and flows into the Trient right below the village Le Trétien. The trail through the gorge starts at the road below the village.

The spring of the Trient torrent is the Trientgletscher (trient glacier) above the village Trient. Along its course between Finhaut and Vernayaz four sections of the gorge are developed with trails.
