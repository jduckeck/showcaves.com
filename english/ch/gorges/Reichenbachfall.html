Description

Reichenbachfall is

Many people know the nearby Reichenbachfall. This waterfall was the location of the death of Sherlock Holmes. The great private investigator was killed by Dr. Moriarty, after a fight in the gorge. Actually this is not in the Aareschlucht, but they are sometimes mixed up, because both are located at Meiringen. The entrance to the Aareschlucht, which is the gorge of the Aare river, is 1.6 km from the funicular in the middle of the valley. The funicular leads uphill on the side of the Aare valley, to a section of an Aare-tributary which has numerous waterfalls and is almost gorge like. And although both sites are actually not connected they are so close by, and easily visited on the same day.
