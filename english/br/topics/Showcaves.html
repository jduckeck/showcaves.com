As you can see, this list contains many more caves than showcaves.com. Unfortunately, we do not know more than the names of most caves. More caves will be added as soon as we get more information. Nevertheless, one thing is obvious: most of the listed objects are definitely not show caves. This is actually a list of cave and karst-related sites which are used for tourism in one way or the other. Only very few have trails or electric light. Even most of the actual show caves require walking in rough terrain and your own lamp, so they are actually semi-wild caves. Some objects are cave trekking sites requiring abseiling and other sportive activities, several are very remote and hard to reach, up to several days of hiking. This applies especially for the sites which are not listed on showcaves.com.
