Caves of Brazil


Tourism has been discovering the beauty and adventure provided by Brazilian caves. Large entrances and interior halls, underground lakes and waterfalls, and the extraordinary beauty of speleothems can be observed in Brazil.

It is quite difficult to find information on tourist caves in Brazil, because publications are in Portuguese, and show caves often do not have any web footprint. Most show caves in Brazil are on the cave trekking, semi-wild side. Caves with trails and electric light are rare, normally you get a torch, a helmet, and a general direction. Cave guides are called monitors and intended to protect the cave (which actually is a good idea, but quite uncommon). Nevertheless there are always lists of show caves or tourist caves, like this one.
