History

1990 area declared a Special Protection Area (APE).
2016 declared a State Natural Monument.

Description

Gruta Lapa Nova (New Cave) or Lapa Nova de Vazante is located east of the town Vazante on a forested hill. It is the 6th largest cave in Minas Gerais. Since 2016 the cave, together with the Lapa Nova 2 and the Lapa da Gameleira, are protected by a 80 ha big Monumento Natural. The cave is visited with guided tours, guides wait at the cave entrance. The cave is not developed except for a rough footpath and some ropes and markers which keep the visitors on track.

At the end of the road R. Lapa Nova is a huge parking lot. Here was a few years ago the restaurant Restaurante Lapa Nova. The trail to the cave starts behind the restaurant, about 200 m uphill. It seems the restaurant is now closed, probably a result of the transformation into a State Natural Monument. The formerly private property where the cave is located was transformed into a public park.
