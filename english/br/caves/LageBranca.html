History

2008 closed for visits due to new law.

Description

Gruta da Lage Branca is most interesting for its entrance portal. It forms a huge shelter, 30 m wide, 50 m high and 20 m deep. The cave was closed in 2008 due to a new law which requires a protection concept. As far as we know it has not yet been reopened.
