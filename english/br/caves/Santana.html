    Description

    Caverna Santana is most beautiful cave of the Petar National Park. It shows beautiful speleothems and is a real maze. Only the first part is accessible to visitors. It is developed with wooden ladders and bridges.

    Spelunking tours are made into some other parts of the cave. To protect the environment, the rest of the cave is only accessible to specialists with an authorization.
