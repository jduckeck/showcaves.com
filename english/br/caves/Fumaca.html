Description

The tour through Gruta da Fumaça (Smoke Cave) is very easy and recommended for all audiences. It has several huge chambers with great speleothems. Highlight is the final hall with a ceiling full of stalactites that are so fine that they resemble a sky of needles. Helmets and lamps are provided.
