History


Description

In the entrance part of the Rzeczka complex, the walls are covered by concrete and look like a sort of underground train station. This part of the system is used as an exhibition and shows maps of all complexes. This complex has the largest chamber, which is 80 meters long and 10 meters high.

This underground structure is a part of Unternehmen Riese which includes the underground factories of Rzeczka, Osówka, and Włodarz.
