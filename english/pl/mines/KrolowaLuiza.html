History

1791 mine opened.
1985 new museum inaugurated.

Description

This historic mine dates from 1791 and in 1985 a new building was erected, especially for visitors, to house an exhibition of mining equipment through the ages. Visitors are shown around the plant and buildings at the pit top, including a 1915 steam hoist. The tour includes a descent to the 35m adit level and ends in the gift shop.
