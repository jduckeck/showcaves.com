History

1854 Bóbrka oil mine opened.
1872 stone obelisk erected by I. Łukasiewicz in the center of the mine to commemorate its foundation.
23-MAY-1961 museum opened to the public.
1982 bust of I. Łukasiewicz unveiled.
2018 declared a Historical Monument.

Description

This museum has probably the longest name of all sites listed on showcaves.com. It is Muzeum Przemysłu Naftowego i Gazowniczego im. Ignacego Łukasiewicza w Bóbrce and it translates "Ignacy Łukasiewicz Museum of Oil and Gas Industry in Bóbrce". But it has also a shorter name, which is Muzeum Nafty i Gazu (Oil and Gas Museum). It is located on the site of Bóbrka oil mine, one of very few mines where crude oil was mined. This mine was founded by Ignacy Łukasiewicz, Tytus Trzecieski and Karol Klobassa-Zrencki.
