History


Description

Rynek Podziemny (Rynek Underground) is located below Rynek Główny, the marketplace of Krakow. It is an underground museum showing the history of the city. Also, archaeological remains like old foundations can be seen. There are also changing exhibitions of various topics. The cellars are located below the town hall, which located in the center of the square.
