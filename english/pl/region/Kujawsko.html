Kujawsko-Pomorskie

Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship


The Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship is situated in mid-northern Poland, on the boundary between the two historic regions Kujawy (Kuyavia) and Pomorze (Pomerania, in German Pommern). Mostly fertile but flat landscape with no underground sights.
