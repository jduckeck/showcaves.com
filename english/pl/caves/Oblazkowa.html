Jaskinia Obłazkowa


Useful Information

Location: Dolina Kościeliska Skrzyżowanie, parking at Kira Leśnicka. 4.4 km, 100 m above Skała Pisana, red trail to Obłazkowa and through the Mylna Cave. One-way route, exit at Hala Pisana. Walking time: 50 minutes.

History

1887 first described by Jan Gwalbert Pawlikowski.
1934 Stefan Zwoliński and Jerzy Zahorski discovered the End Hall by making a 5 m long ditch in the corridor.
2013-2014 resurvey of the caves in the National Park.
JUN-2014 Mylna Cave connected with Obłazkowa Cave via Jędrusiów Przełaz.
FEB-2015 Left Corridor of the Raptawicka Cave connected to the corridor above the Connection Chimney in the Mylna Cave.
