Oldest Showcaves of Poland


This is a ranking of show caves in Poland by the year in which they were opened to the public.


Year Name Explanation
1842 Smocza Jama Located right in the center of the Voivodeship capital Kraków (Cracow), it is easy to understand why it is the oldest show cave of the country.
1870 Jaskinia Wierzchowska Górna The huge natural entrance was known for a long time, the cave was visited by naturalists since the 19th century.

1935 Jaskinia Radochowska
1953 Jaskinia Mroźna
1972 Jaskinia Raj
1983 Jaskinia Niedźwiedzia
1987 Jaskinia Łokietka
1992 Jaskinia Ciemna
1994 Jaskinia Nietoperzowa
2005 Jaskinia Głęboka
2012 Jaskinie na Kadzielni
