History

1991 opened to the public.
1999 inscribed into the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Description

Wondercave consists only of a single huge chamber. Visitors enter this chamber by an 40m descent with an elevator from the surface. The cave has many formations, including rimstone pools, cave pearls, and popcorn formations. The stalagmites are up to 15m high.
