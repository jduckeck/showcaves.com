The park is artificial, the building is artificial, the limestone walls on the outside are as artificial as the cave inside. Its plastic, plaster and colour. It's a pretty well done cave replica though, but it is neither old nor natural. And it is an exhibition with religious content, mostly so-called "miracles", hence the name, of the prophets from the old testament.
