History

2019 cave replica created.

Description

كهف العجائب (Dahl Al Eajayib, Cave of Wonders) is an artificial cave which is located in the Museum Park called Heritage Garden at the National Museum of Qatar. It was created to allow especially children to explore a cave, as the number of cave in Qatar is quite limited. Unfortunately the cave is not very realistic, some visitors even noticed the air conditioning: cave is Qatar are actually quite warm. But nevertheless it is a nice experience for children, it’s primarily a playground not an exhibition.
