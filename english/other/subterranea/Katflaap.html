Description

The Catacombs of Katflaap, sometimes also called the Catacombs of St. Katflaap, are located in the village Katflaap in the eastern Steppes. The catacombs are today a popular wine bar. The specialty are local wines from the Chateau Sultàna region and the local specialty Zeerstum (garlic vodka).
