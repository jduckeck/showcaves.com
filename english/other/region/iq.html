Iraq


Iraq, located in the Middle East, is hardly a tourist country. Actually, it does a lot to keep foreigners away. This includes frequent wars, terrorism, civil unrest, and a disfunctioning government. And climate change is rapidly desertifying arable land. As a result we actually have no sites we could list, nevertheless, we finally decided to add the country.
