Description

Zion is the last free city of mankind. Deep inside earth, in a huge cave, lies a city with the last free humans on Earth. All other people are part of the Matrix and used by the machines to produce energy. The city is built in an artificially created cavern, but the temple is a natural cave, with nice speleothems like stalactites and stalagmites.
