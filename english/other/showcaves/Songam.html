Description

송암동굴 (Songam Cave) is a modern show cave with state of the art electric light. It is a horizontal passage with a series of 17 chambers, which show different types of speleothems and even an underground waterfall. For some weird reason they are quite fond of their air conditioning. We are not sure if there is actually air conditioning or if this is a metaphor for the cave being cooler than outside. But if they really installed air conditioning, it’s a great danger to the cave.
