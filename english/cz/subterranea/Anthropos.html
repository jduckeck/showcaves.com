Description

The Moravian Museum has an exhibition located in the Pisarky Park, west of the center of Brno, which is called Pavilon Anthropos. Lately renovated, it is an impressive exhibition of the development of man. Various connections with caves can be found, caves used as shelter, dioramas of our ancestors living in cave entrances, cave animals, discoveries from caves of the Czech Republic, and of course cave paintings. The museum has an enormous collection of cave painting replicas from all over the world.
