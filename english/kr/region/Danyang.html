Danyang-gun

District Danyang



The city of Danyang is the capital of a small district of the same name right in the middle of the Korean Peninsula. The karst area around the city has four interesting caves which are open to the public.


  • Ch'ondong Gul
  • Gosudong Gul
  • Nodong Gul
  • Ondaldong Gul
