Gyeonggi-do

Gyeonggi Province


Gyeonggi-do is located in the northwest corner of South Korea. Its neighbours are Gangwon on the east, North and South Chungcheong on the south, and Incheon on the west. To the west is some coastline of the Yellow Sea. The province almost completely surrounds Seoul, the Capital of South Korea, which has its own province. To the north lies North Korea and the DMZ, bordering Kaesong Industrial Region and North Hwanghae Province.
