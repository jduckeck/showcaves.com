History

 
2003 cave discovered.
SEP-2006 opens to the public.

Description

Mulgol Cave is a very recent discovery in the area of the cave rich city Daei-ri. 50 caves are known in the city, this is the second to be open to the public, following Hwanseon Cave. The river cave is very rich in water, the tour starts with an eight metre high waterfall, right behind the entrance.
