History

1993 developed as show cave.

Description

Haan Gul was discovered during quarry works. It consists mainly of an enormous chamber of 200 x 50m and some small passages.

This is a sort of mystery cave, as there existed only a singe page on the web which described the cave. It was a prive page by a Japanese tourist who visited the cave and tokk lots of pictures. There was no sign of a scam, but probably he mixed up the name somehow. Also he did not provide any helpful info except his pictures, no address or location. And when performing a web search there is no trace of a cave named Haan Gul. At the moment our best guesses are, this is actually one of the other caves we listed and he mixed up the name, or the cave has been closed to the public since. Unfurtunately even the orginal page with the pictures is down now.
