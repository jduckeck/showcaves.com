    History

     
    FEB-1977 discovered by an inhabitant of Cheondong village.
    08-DEC-1977 cave designated regional monument No. 19.

    Description

    Cheondong cave, named after the village it is located at, is a very recent discovery. It was found in 1977 by a local and declared a National Monument in the same year. It is located between Pirobong Peak and YonHwabong Peak, the main peaks of Mt. Sobaeksan.

    Only 470m in length this cave is famous for the delicacy and beauty of its speleothems.
