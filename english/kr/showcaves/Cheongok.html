History

24-JUN-1991 cave discovered when the foundations of the new city hall were built.

Description

The main specialty of this cave is its location: right in the middle of the big city Donghae. It was discovered when the foundations of the new city hall were built. As it is located in the city it is very popular among the locals and an important destination for school classes. It is well developed and a modern cave museum satisfies the didactic needs of the cave visitors.
