History

1980 underground mall built with bus terminal.
2011 Goto Mall Co., Ltd. established.
2012 mall renovated.

Description

고투몰 (Goto Mall) is probably the largest underground mall in South Korea, with 620 shops and an area of 31,566 m². It is located in the basement of Express Bus Terminal Station, which is huge and covers a whole city block. It connects the bus terminal with four subway lines, has 16 exits and a passage to Banpo Station, and is thus a major interchange station. Its actual name or full name is 강남터미널지하쇼핑몰 (Gangnam Terminal Underground Shopping Mall), Goto is the abbreviation and nickname.
