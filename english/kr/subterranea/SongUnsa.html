Description

대한불교 조계종송운사 (Korean Buddhism Jogye Order Song Unsa) is a set of cave temples built into the abandoned amethyst quarries. The temples are well maintained and in operation, respect their religion and behave, then you may visit for free and even take a few pictures. This is not a tourist site, the tourist site is only 100 m away. It is a temple of the Jogye Order of Korean Buddhism named Song Unsa. The abandoned mines are also named 송운사미타대석굴 (Songun Samita Grottoes).
