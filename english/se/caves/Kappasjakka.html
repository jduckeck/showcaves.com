Description

Kåppasjåkka is about 2,300 meters long, which makes it Sweden's 5th longest cave. Cave tours are organized during winter, when the cave is reached on snowshoes, a dog sled or snowmobile. The karst cave has stalactites and stalagmites, and a cave river.
