History

1985 discovered by a group of cavers from the Swedish Speleological Society.

Description

Korallgrottan is noted as it is the longest cave in Sweden. It is a limestone cave system with more than 4km length. It is not a show cave and the entrance is closed with a massive iron gate. But there are guided cave trekking tours into the cave.
