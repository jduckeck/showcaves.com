History

 
1969 a strike at Leveäniemi spread to other mines in nearby Kiruna and Mamberget.

Description

Leveäniemigruvan is well known for its iron phosphate minerals. Very common are strengite, beraunite, rockbridgeite and a number of different zeolites. The mine may be visited and it is possible to collect minerals, which is rather unusual.
