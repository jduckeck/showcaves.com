Bihar - Bihār


Bihar is a state in eastern India. Musch of it is the Bihar plain which is split by the river Ganges, flowing from west to east. This area is devoid of natural caves and because of the rather instable underground almost no artificial caves. There is a tiny part of the Himalaya which belongs to this state in the northwestern tip, and hillcountry along the southern border. That's where the few caves can be found.
