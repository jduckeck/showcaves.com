Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu


Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu is in our opinion a good example for human egocentric nonsense and bureaucracy. They united four small pieces of land which are not connected and some are even islands to form a State. The territory is made up of four separate geographical entities: Dadra, Nagar Haveli, Daman, and the island of Diu. The reason why they did this was simply because all four were formerly part of Portuguese India. That’s the reason why they all love each other and all had to be part of the name.
