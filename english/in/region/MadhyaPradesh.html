Madhya Pradesh


Madhya Pradesh translates Central Province, and actually it is a landlocked province in the center of India. It has three major seasons, Summer (March to June), Monsoon (June to September), and Winter (October to February). The summers are extremely hot and Monsoon extremely rainy, so winter is probably the best season for travelling here.
