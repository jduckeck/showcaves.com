History


Description

Guhantara is a hotel or resort, but obviously not a common one, otherwise we would not list it. This resort was created by the two architects S N Ramesh and C H Ramesh, who invented a sort of artificial cave, complete with underground waterfall and stone age drawings on the walls. Guhantara is Sanskrit and translates inside of a cave.
