Vorarlberg


Vorarlberg is the westernmost federal state and borders Switzerland and Germany. It lies in the Northern Limestone Alps and has several karst areas and gorges. Mining was also practised here. However, there is not a single show cave and only one show mine.

  • Engenlochschlucht
  • Quelltuffpfad Lingenau
  • Rappenlochschlucht
