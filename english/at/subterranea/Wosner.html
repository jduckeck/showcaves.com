Description

Erdstall im Gasthof Wösner is not a proper name, it is a description. Through an opening in the cellar of the Wösner inn you can enter an Erdstall. As is often the case with earth barns, it is located in the immediate vicinity of the church. A visit to the Erdstall is possible by appointment, we assume during opening hours and not when it is busy. We are not completely sure if the visit is free, however, this is an inn, if you visit the Erdstall, have a meal and a beer.
