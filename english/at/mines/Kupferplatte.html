Schaubergwerk Kupferplatte

Copper Plate Mine


Useful Information

History

1874 smelting at Jochberg ended.
31-JUL-1926 mining at Jochberg ended.
1990 show mine opened.

Description

The copper at the Kupferplatte was mined even 3,000 years ago. In the middle of the 19th century 160 miners worked here and produced 1,800 tons of copper ore every year.
