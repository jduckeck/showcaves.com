History

 
1861 mine closed.
1991 reopened as a show mine.

Description

The Erlebnisbergwerk Lehenlahn offers various attractions, like the Medieval passages, information about the work and the life of the miners, displays with Gezähe (miners tools) and different mining technologies. But there are also fairy tales for children. The mine tour emphasizes on entertainment, information about mining is only the backbone.
