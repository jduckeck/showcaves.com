History

1960s Flurite mined.
1989 showmine opened to the public.

Description

The fluorit mine in Vorderkrimml is more like a mineral museum than a classic show mine. It concentrates on the local fluorites and has a chamber with ultraviolet light, where he flourites glow in eerie colours.
