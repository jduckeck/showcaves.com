History


Description

The Kurzentrum Bad Bleiberg is one of five spas in Austria which have united. But this is the only one of the five which offers speleotherapy. The location for this are three parts of the abandoned lead mines around Bad Bleiberg: Heilklimastollen Rudolfsschacht, Zeche Sabine and Zeche Barbara. They are part of the spa, which offers accomodation, wellness, various therapies and more. The speleotherapy includes the supervision by specifically trained personell. The special offer at this institution are trainings in the mine for correct respiration.

This is not a show mine, it is exclusively a mine for speleotherapy, and it is not possible to visit this mine except for therapy.
