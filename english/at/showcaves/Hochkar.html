History

1969 opened to the public.

Description

The location of the Hochkarschacht is rather special. This cave is on top of a mountain right in the middle of a ski resort. To get there we take a steep toll road which goes up to a plateau right below the summit of the Hochkar (1,808m asl). Here is a huge parking, several hotels and restaurants, the cable car to the summit and ski lifts. Most of the year this ski resort is deserted. But in winter, with enough snow, it is a popular ski area and overcrowded each weekend by day tourists.

The Hochkarschacht is a huge alpine cave. Main sight is the 100 m deep entrance shaft, which is really impressive. But the visitor enters the cave at the floor, through an artificial adit which leads dircetly to the vast Whitsun Hall, a chamber 50m long and 20m wide. The cave is noteworthy for its series of huge wide vaults linked by small narrow passages.

Unfortunately it is rather difficult to visit this cave. Although it is well developed, with good paths, entrance adits and electric light, there are no regular tours. It is necessary to make an appointment andthe minimum group size is nine persons. There is absolutely no chance to get in without prior arrangement.
