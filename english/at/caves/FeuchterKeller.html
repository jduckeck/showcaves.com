History

1923 expedition into the cave aborted because of the high risk of rockfall.
1981-82 exploration and survey by Roth, Gadermayer, Knapcyk.
1984 discovery of new cave parts at the bottom of 32m deep shaft (Hecht, Gadermayer, Strasset).

Description

The Feuchter Keller (Wet Cellar) is a daylight shaft leading into a cave system. This cave tour is a true caving expedition offered by the Höhlenrettungsdienst Salzburg (Cave Rescue Salzburg) for people interested in high level caving trips. It is not really a difficult trip, but there are definitely no trails, and beneath full caving gear some vertical experience and surefootedness is necessary. However, the guys from the cave rescue are the right bunch to get anybody back to the surface, one way or another.
