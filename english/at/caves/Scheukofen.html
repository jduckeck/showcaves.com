Scheukofen

Scheukofenhöhle


Useful Information

History

1828 visited by Ferdinand Raimund.
1975 deadly accident, two cave divers die.

Description

The Scheukofen is a nice cave with speleothems and some easy climbs. Some low parts make the cave an adverture for children. However, it is not a show cave, you will get dirty, should be reasonable fit and have no problems with tight passages. Participants should bring wellingtons, working gloves, old clothes, Schlaz (caving overall).

The cave is about 1 h walk from the parking lot. The 4 hours tour will include little height difference, just a view easy climbs. Some Schlufe (low passages) give the tour some adventure. The cave has nice speleothems.
