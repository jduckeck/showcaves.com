1986-1992 trail built.
2008 outlook errected.

Description

The Wasserlochklamm is a rather young venture, it was developed from 1986 to 1992 by the town Palfau. Most other gorges in Austria were developed more than a century ago. The gorge starts with a 65m long bridge, followed by numerous stairs, wooden bridges and elevated trails. The five waterfalls have a total height of 152m and at the end is a huge karst spring from which the river emerges.
