Description

The Groppenstein- und Rabisch-Schlucht, or better the Groppensteinschlucht and the Rabisch-Schlucht are two gorges which connect the two National Park municipalities of Mallnitz and Obervellach and together form the longest gorge trail in Carinthia. The gorges together are 5.5 km long with 550 m height difference with up to 30 m high waterfalls. You can take the bus back to the entrance at Obervellach. There is also a round trip which is 13 km long and has a total height difference of 1100 m, which equal about 4.5 hours walking time. The gorges are also called Sinnesschluchten (Sensory Gorges) and inspire with romantic landslide sceneries and fantastic waterfalls. The gorges are located below the picturesque Burg Groppenstein (Groppenstein Castle).
