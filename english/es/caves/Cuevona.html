Description

The cave Cuevona is a huge through cave which was used as a road tunnel. The road built through the cave is the only way to reach the small town Cuevas de Aqua. The road through the cave is about 250m long and rather narrow, the cave is lighted by orange road lights. Obviously there is not enough time to see the cave when just driving through so we recommend a walk. Unfortunately there is no sidewalk, so be careful for the traffic. Although this road is only leading to one small village there are cars, bicyclists and even lorries.
