Description

The Cova Blava is a 40 m long and 20 m high sea cave at the cliff coast of Cabrera island. It is visited by boat. To the south is a second sea cave called Cueva Azul (Blue Grotto). The sea caves can only be visited by boat and there are various cruises offering daytrips to the park.
