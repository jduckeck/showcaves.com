History

1985 area declared a Natural Park.

Description

The Cueva de la Galiana is a beginners cave in this karst area of the Cañón del Río Lobos, which is a Natural Park since 1985. The cave is protected and closed. It is possible to visit the cave on spelunking tours offered by Soriaventura under the name Galiana Undergroud. They provide a cave overall, helmet with head lamp and a guide. It is a good idea to bring comfortable clothes, clothes to change, wellingtons and a towel. The cave has a pleasant temperature, there is no need for warm clothes.

there are two tours offert. The beginners tour does not require any climbing and is suitable for anyone. Children must be at least 10 years old. The adventure tour includes some climbs and takes longer. There must be at least five participants. All tours are made only after appointment.
