History


Description

Senda del Oso (trail of the bear) is popular trail through a narrow gorge with tunnels and impressive views. Originally it was a railroad track, but the miners railroad was abandoned and the tracks removed. The broad trail with its low ascend is an ideal bicycle trail and very popular.
