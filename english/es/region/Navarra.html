Navarra

Comunidad Foral de Navarra - Nafarroako Foru Komunitatea- Navarre


Navarra is a Basque province in northern Spain, at the western part of the Pyrenees, and lanlocked, although the northwestern tipis only a few kilometers from the sea. Located in the transition zone between Green Spain and semi-arid interior areas, its landscapes and climate vary widely across the region.
