Eivissa

Ibiza


Eivissa (Ibiza) is one of the Balearic Islands located in the Mediterranean Sea. Eivissa is the official Catalan name and the name used by its inhabitants, but the rest of the world including Castilian Spain says Ibiza. It is derived from the original Arabic word Yabisah, meaning land or landward in English.

Ibiza is famous for its summer parties, with thousands of foreign guests, including many VIPs. This is probably the result of the mediterranean climate with even milder winters and slightly cooler summers than Mallorca. Ibiza is a comfortable destination all year and famous for its beaches.
