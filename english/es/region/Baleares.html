Islas Baleares

Ibiza - Mallorca - Menorca


The Baleares are several islands in the Mediterranean Sea. The four biggest Islands are

  • Mallorca
  • Menorca
  • Ibiza
  • Formentera

The Baleares are the main beach holiday location for the Europeans. The climate is warm all over the year although in winter there may be some rains. The mild climate makes the islands also a preferred old age residence for seniors from central Europe.
