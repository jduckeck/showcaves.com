History

1914 opened to the public, first show cave in Spain.
1987 surveyed by the G.E.S of S.E.M..

Description

This Grutas de las Maravillas (Cave of Wonders) is an extraordinary decorated cave. Huge halls and passages decorated with all the speleothem you expect: stalagmites, stalactites, straws and shawls. Some of the chambers have pools and the 70 m high salón del gran lago (hall of the great lake) contains a big subterranean lake.

The cave is lighted by indirect light and a sound system allows the playback of music. They play the sinfonía del agua by the local musician Luis de Pablo and the suiteGruta de las Maravillas by Don Primitivo Lázaro.

The system is located is a small outcrop of lower Cambrian marble, limestone which was metamorphized by high pressure and temperature. It forms a hill in the middle of the city Aracena, with the Castelo de Aracena on top.
