Description

The Cuevona de Ardines is located close to the more famous Cueva de Tito Bustillo. It does not contain famous paintings but has fine speleothems. The huge cavern is up to 40 m high. The Centro de Arte Rupestre Tito Bustillo is located in front of the cave entrance right at the road. The visit is actually a little hard to book, as the visit to the art center is free, but the ticket does not include the cave tour. There are guided tours through the museum which are not free, but do not include the cave. And then there are cave tickets which are also not free, but include the museum. Both are open at the same times, while the nearby Tito Bustillo cave has different times and different tickets.
