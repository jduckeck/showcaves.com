History


Description

The Parc Prehistòric de les Coves del Toll de Moià contains four caverns in total In the caves flint tools between 100,000 and 40,000 years old were found. On the tour of the caves, visitors learn about everyday life of prehistoric man, how they crafted tools, built fires, hunted, made pottery, and more.
