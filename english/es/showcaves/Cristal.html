History

1981 opened to the public.

Description

The crystals mentioned in the name of Grutas de Cristal are helictites. The cave is famous for huge amounts of helictites all over walls and ceiling.

Another important discovery in the cave were the remains of the Hombre de Molinos (Molinos Man).
