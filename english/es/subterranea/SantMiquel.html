History

10th cty monastery built.

Description

The monastery Sant Miquel del Fai was built during the 10th century around the church of Sant Miquel. The location is unique and quite spectacular, it is located on a rocky ledge between two waterfalls. A group of cliffs, known as Les Cingles de Bertí, forms the escarpment at the end of the valley. The rivers Rossinyol and Tenes flow over the escarpment forming waterfalls. In the middle of the cliff face a wide ledge was formed by a hard layer of limestone due to differential erosion.
