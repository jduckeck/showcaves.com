History

1972 Don José Martín Quesada and Doña Concepción Maya.

Description

Cueva los Tarantos is a traditional zambra cave and restaurant. They are very proud of the quality of their show.

This cave has been for years a breeding ground for dancers, guitarists and singers who have achieved successful careers in the world of flamenco, with national and international recognition. Through our stage have passed the best artists that Granada has seen. Today the establishment has three different caves for flamenco shows.

There is not much we can add. This is the zambra cave which is closest to the city, so it is faster and easier to reach. They also offer pickup service from the hotel, which is very convenient, and probably much cheaper than a taxi. However, they do not have the view the caves high on the hillside have.
