Description

La Morería is located at Les Coves de Vinromà. While there are actually natural caves, the are not called Les Coves de Vinromà, this is the name of the village. And right beneath the village is a rock with artificial caves, cave houses and storage rooms, which were constructed during the Moorish occupation of Spain.
