History

1971 bar opened.

Description

El Chapandáz is a cave bar, which means it is decorated like a natural dripstone cave. Walls and ceiling are covered by artificial stalagmites and stalactites. The specialties of the bar are cocktails with milk, and so the center of the bar contains a sort of milk fountain: milk is coming out of an artificial stalactite above the bar. The most famous cocktail of the bar its the Leche di Pantera (Panther's Milk).
