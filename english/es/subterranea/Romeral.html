History

1800 b.C. built and in use.

Description

The Cueva de Romeral is one of three prehistoric cave tombs at Antequera. It was built around 1800 b.C..

This cave was a surprise for the archaeologists, as it posesses a dome, built in a special technique which is known only from Greece. They speculated about connections to greece 4000 years ago.
