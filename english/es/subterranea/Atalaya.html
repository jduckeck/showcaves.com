Description

Cuevas Atalaya is a series of cave houses which are located on different levels on a gentle slope. Originally they were inhabited, each cave house was home to a family, now all those houses were transformed into hotel rooms. The former city quarter is now a hotel. The rooms are equipped with modern amenities like TV and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, microwave, stove, toaster and coffee maker. Each room has a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Each room has its own terrace, and there are a seasonal pool, barbecue area and free private parking. Free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas.
