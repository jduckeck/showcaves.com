Description

The area around Guadix is famous for thousands of cave houses. The easiest way to see one and actually have a try living in a cave is the comfortable Hotel Cuevas Pedro Antonio de Alarcón in Guadix. It offers high level infrastructure and the comfort of a cool 19 °C cave.
