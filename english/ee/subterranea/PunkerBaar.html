Bunker is a Cold war Museum and Art Gallery

A Cold War museum/gallery in a WWII bomb shelter just outside the Old Town of Tallinn. Cold War memorabilia, styled in the aesthetic of post-apocalyptic Soviet nuclear shelters, and frequently changing historical photo exhibitions.
