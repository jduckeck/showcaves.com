History

1630s first bastion erected.

Description

The Narva Victoria Bastioni kasematid (Casemates of the Bastion Victoria in Narva) are located under the Victoria Bastion. So when you visit the bastion you should definitely take a tour through the casemates. The first level of the casemates is used for an exhibition and is accessible during the open hours of the bastion. The other parts are only accessible during a guided tour.

The Narva Victoria bastion was never conquered, although the country was in war several times in the 300 years of its existence.
