History

1884 discovered by Gregor Žiberna.
1987 developed and opened to the public.

Description

Divaška Jama is one of the youngest show caves of Slowenia. It was developed in 1987 by the Jamarski Drustvo Gregor Žiberna (Caving Club Gregor Žiberna). This club is named after Gregor Žiberna, nickname Tentava, who discovered the cave in 1884.
