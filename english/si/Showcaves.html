This is the official show caves list of Slovenia from the Karst Institute in Postojna. One of the caves has since been closed forever. The caves which are not linked are not listed on showcaves.com because they are too dangerous. We are not sure why they are on this list, because all of them require climbing equipment and a guide. Probably they were used for cave trekking tours.
