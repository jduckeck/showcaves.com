Description

The Parco Fluviale Novella is the gorge of the river Novella. It is a normal gorge with an elevated trail, but also offering kayaking and canyoning. The walking tour is guided and takes two hours. The guides tell a lot about the geology, biology, and history of the gorge. As the number of visitors is not restricted reservation is not required. The kayak and canyoning tours are restricted and reservation is required.
