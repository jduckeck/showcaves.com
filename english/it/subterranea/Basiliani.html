History

1993 inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Description

Located in Matera, overlooking the Gravina gorge, this is an exceptional set of artificial, historic caves which was carefully transformed into a design hotel. Unlike all the other hotels, this caves are not restored to be as original as possible. This caves are modernizes with modern design furniture and prove that the caves are actually a pretty modern concept.
