Description

Il Borgo Ritrovato is a hotel category which is called albergo diffuso in Italian. This means a series of historic houses or caves has been converted into a modern hotel. Numerous houses with originally different owners give the albergo diffuso a character like a historic village or at least quarter transfored into a musem.

The hotel has its own restaurant Il Trappeto, equipped with a historic oil press.
