Description

Criptoportico Forense (Forensic Cryptoporticus) is a horseshoe or U shaped underground passage with barrel vaulted openings in the middle of the passage separating it into two parallel passages. The site is quite exceptional and obviously of Roman origin, and it is located in the middle of the Roman district of the city Aosta, between the Roman theater and the ruins of the Roman city wall and baths.
