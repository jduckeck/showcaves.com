History

1993 inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Description

Caveoso Hotel is named after nearby Piazza San Pietro Caveoso, right in the centre of the Sassi district. The original cave house, a striking historic palace, has been renovated with care to maintain its original character. The stylish furniture offers an elegant accommodation.
