History

42-51 constructed during the reign of Claudius.

Description

Cunicoli di Claudio (Tunnels of Claudius) are located at the foot of Monte Salviano, south of the city of Avezzano. By the locals they are often called Cunicoli di Nerone (Tunnels of Nero), but that's a misnomer. Its only a single tunnel, and it was constructed between 42 and 51 AD during the reign of Claudius.

The tunnel was the first attempt to drain Lake Fucino.
