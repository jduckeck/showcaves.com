Rimini


Rimini is well know for its beaches. The east coast of Italy is flat and sandy. This means hundreds of kilometers of beach, a paradise for sun lovers. Sometimes the area is called Teuton grill, as so many Germans (still) spend their holiday here, lying in the sun and getting redder day by day....

Not one in thousand visitors knows about the numerous underground sights of the area. Beneath the natural caves there are interesting subterranea from the long history since Roman times.
