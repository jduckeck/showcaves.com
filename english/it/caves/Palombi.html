Description

Grotta dei Palombi (Cave of the Pigeons), in Sardinian Gruta dels Coloms, is located on the small island of Foradada, which translates ‘pierced’. Actualy the cave is a through-cave crossing the island from the east side to the west side. It is not a sea cave though, it is a few meters above the sea level and dry.

So while the cave is actually visited by walking, the only way to access it is by boat. There are boat operators departing from the port of Alghero, which actually travel to the nearby show cave Grotta di Nettuno. There is also a viewpoint at SS 55 named Belvedere Foradada, which allows a view on the island and the eastern entrance of the cave.
