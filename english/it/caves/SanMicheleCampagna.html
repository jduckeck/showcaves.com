Description

The Eremo di San Michele (hermitage of St Michael) is a small Benedictine monastery built into an overhanging cliff of the Monti Picentini. It is far from roads, the only way to reach it is by hiking, for example from Puglietta, which is actually a part of Campagna. The hermitage is located about 1,000m asl, so it is quite an ascent from the 400m asl high village.
