History

1896 first explored.
1949 explorers reach the bottom of the Vigant abyss.
1965 sump which connects two caves first dived.
1977-78 cave explored and new passages discovered.
27-07-1996 trail inaugurated.

Description

Abisso Vigant is a rather famous vertical cave, and it is not posible to visit this cave without climbing equipment. What we want to present on this page is not primarily the cave itself, but the nature trail which was built into the entrance of the cave. The massive cave portal is accessible on a broad and comfortable path with a wooden railing. The trail only shows the entrance part of the cave, which is even electrically lighted. There is an informative plate, which explains the geography and geology with surface and cave maps. It is also known as Olobigniza (small mouth).
