History

1797 discovered by an hunter, name unknown.
1892 developed, electric light and opened to the public.
1917 installation more or less ruined.
1966 reconstruction of show cave, reopened.

Description

Grotta dei Dossi is a show cave with a long history. Opened at the end of the 19th century, it was the first cave with electric light in Italy. The electricity was produced by a generator. But soon forgotten, the cave was already ruined at the time of World War I. Long time the cave was closed, until it was reopened in the 1960s.

The more fragile speleothems were removed, stolen or destroyed in the long history of the cave, but some huge stalactites, stalagmites and rimstone pools are still there. But the cave is today famous for its diverse and fragile cave life.
