History


Description

Cueva del Infierno or Cueva Boca del Infierno (Hellmouth Cave) is located in the village of Hatillo. It is a river cave which is famous for its rimstone pools. The cave is one of the entrances to the Rio Camuy system. The cave was guided on cave trekking tours by Tanama Tours, but currently they offer no tours.
