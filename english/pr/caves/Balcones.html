Description

Cueva Balcones is not easily accessed, it requires a hike through the rain forest. We have no exact location and the coordinates given by various geo sites on the web are all wrong. This is actually a good thing, as it is essential to visit the cave with a guide. And the guide will know the way. This is a wild cave, but it is mostly horizontal and does not require climbing or swimming. It has beautiful speleothems. The cave is guided by José Abraham Bruno Cordero, but as far as we know he is not online, so it is probably best to ask at your hotel or the tourist office.
