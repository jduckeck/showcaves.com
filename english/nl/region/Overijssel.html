Overijssel


Overijssel (Upper IJssel) means across the Ijssel and actually means on the German side of the river. The whole region is covered by loose sediments like sand, gravel, and clay. One third was originally bogs, which were dried mostly during the 19th century. At least it is not as low as other regions, the higest mountain is 85 m asl. Nevertheless, not much underground.
