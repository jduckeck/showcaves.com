History

2002 opened to the public.

Description

The Historische Kelders Arnhem (Historic Cellars of Arnhem) are 39 different cellars located along Rijnstraat, the central road through the medieval town center. The cellars date back to the 13th, 14th and 15th century. Today a small part is open to the public. They are used as art museum, for christmas parties and for rock concerts.

Under the historic Sint Petersgasthuis (Hotel Saint Peter) is a huge celler which is used for all kinds of event. It may be booked for company meetings, weddings, christening, and birthday parties.
