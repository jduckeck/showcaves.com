History

28-APR-1983 Museum opened to the public.

Description

The Nederlands Wijnmuseum (Dutch Wine Museum) is located in Arnhem underground, in a historic wine cellar. It was founded more than 20 years ago by Michael Broadbent, wine auctioneer of Christie's, and Mollie Geertsema, the legendary Commissaris der Koningin (Chief of police of the Queen). The content of the museum is obvious: wine of all ages and sorts. There are explanations on wine growing and wine making, and an overview on Netherlands wines. There are also abundant possibilities to taste wine.
