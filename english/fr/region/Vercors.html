Vercors


The Vercors is a region of France, a part of the région Rhône-Alpes and located in the two départements of Isère and Drôme in Eastern France. The Vercors is also called Vercors Plateau, as it is a plateau formed by the French Prealps. It is separated by the rivers Drac and Isère from the Dauphiné Alps to the west.
