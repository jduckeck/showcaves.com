Description

Moët & Chandon is one of the most important champagne houses. Thomas Jefferson was born in the founding year of the vinery, and he was a lifelong fan of their champagne. In general the Unites States are an important market for this champagne for centuries now.

The celar tour starts at the company's headquarter, a yellow-brick house in the center of Epernay.
