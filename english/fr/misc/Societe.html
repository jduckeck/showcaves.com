History

 
17th cty cellars built.
1863 company founded.

Description

The company Société des Caves de Roquefort produces roquefort cheese in old cellars. They have different titles under which they sell cheese, on is titles 1863 after the year the company was founded. Another one is named Baragnaudes after the oldest cellars from the 17th century called Caves Baragnaudes. Baragnaudes is the Occitan word for fairies, and this is the cellar which is the most natural one.
