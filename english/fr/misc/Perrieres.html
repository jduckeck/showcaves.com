History

 
1935 quarries closed.
1955 mushroom farm opened.
1970 mushroom farm closed.
1989 Centre des Perrières opened to the public.

Description

Perrières is the local term for limestone quarries. Huge areas of the city Doué la Fontaine are hollowed out by the work of centuries. This museum of the quarries and their development is located inside an 18th to early 20th century quarry. It contains exhibitions, art expositions and educational facilities. There is a hostel with 50 beds and a reception area seating 70 guests which may be booked for weddings or other feasts.
