Route des Fortifications Européenes


The Route des Fortifications Européenes (Route of European Fortifications) is a group of military structures located in the Grand Est Region. The non-profit association which managed them have forme this new association which spans 150 years of French and German fortification construction. The topic is Franco-German, but its influence is at least European or probably even world history.
