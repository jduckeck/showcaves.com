Sentier Karstique


It seems France is the country with the most karst trails in the world. They are called Sentier Karstique in French. As far as we know there is no complete list of the existing trails, except ours. Some lists show eight or even eleven trails, probably different ones. This is the list of all trails we know of. Like always we can not guarantee its complete.
