Description

Mines de Fer de Bourbach-le-Bas (Iron Mines of Bourbach-le-Bas) is a 6 km long mining and archaeology themed walk around the village. Clothe appropriately for the walk, sun protection, raincoat, good walking shoes are required. Bring food and water. The walk includes the visit of a 400 m long mine gallery. Helmets and lamps for the mine gallery are provided.
