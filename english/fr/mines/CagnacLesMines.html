History

12th century earliest coal mining.
1810 mining company Carmaux founded.
1890 Mines of Albi founded.
1946 mines nationalized.
1960s begin of decline.
1987 last undergroung mine closed.
1989 mining museum opened.
1997 last open cast mine closed.

Geology


Description

The Musée de la Mine Cagnac les Mines documents the 700 years of coal mining history of this departement. A 350 m long artificial mine passage is guided like a show mine, visitors get helmets. It displays all steps necessary to mine coal.
