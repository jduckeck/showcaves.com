History

1917 mine closed.
1994 mining museum opened to the public.
1995 mine under protection by the Conservatoire du Littoral (French coastal protection agency).

Geology


Description

The Musée de la Mine de Cap Garonne is located at an extraordinary location, not far from the Mediterranean coast. It is dedicated to the local copper mining.
