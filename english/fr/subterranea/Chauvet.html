History


Description

This is the local exhibition to the nearby Grotte Chauvet. As the original cave is closed to visitors, to protect the paintings, this nearby museum allows interested visitors to see at least a very good replica of the most important parts of the cave. The exhibition was made by the Museum National d'Histoire Naturelle de Paris.

The exhibion includes three rooms with explanations about the live of Cro Magnon man in the Ardeche valley and twoo rooms with a replica of the most important paintings and engravings of the cave. A 25min. long video film explains the fact known about the cave by now.
