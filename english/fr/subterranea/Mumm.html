History

1827 founded.

Description

The general visit is called Cordon Rouge and includes a 10min film and a visit to the cellars. It is named after the G.H. Mumm Cordon Rouge, the House’s signature champagne. Obviously it also includes the tasting of this champagne, at least for those of legal age.

The G.H. Mumm Caveau or cellar is available for receptions and meetings. It has 300m² and seats up to 180 guests, up to 280 on cocktail receptions.
