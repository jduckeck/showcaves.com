Description

The visit to the Celliers De Castellane includes also a visit to the Museum of Champagne Crafts, the Printing Museum, and the Poster collection. The Tower is 66 m high, has eight floors and 237 steps, and is listed in the Inventory of Historic Buildings. It is visited on the longer Tower Tour, which also offers two glasses of wine. And finally there is a private guided Tour for a maximum of four guests, called Heritage Tour. It includes La Salle des Etiquettes and its 7000 drawers preserving a collection of 10,000 labels and champagne collars, from the 19th century until today. For this tour booking is required and there are only two tours daily.
