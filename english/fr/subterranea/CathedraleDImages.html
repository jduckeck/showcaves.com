History

1975 quarries first visited by Albert Plécy.
1977 Albert Plécy died, work continued by his wife Anne Plécy.

Description

Cathedrale d’Images is a modern use for an old quarry. It is somewhere between an art exhibition and a multimedia artwork. Created by the artist Albert Plécy, it shows exhibitions in an absolutely new way. The pictures are projected on the white walls of the former quarry.
