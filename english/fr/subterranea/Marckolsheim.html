History


Description

The Musée Mémorial de la Ligne Maginot du Rhin (Memorial Museum of the Rhine Maginot Line) is located in the Kasematte 35/3 Marckolsheim. The small village is located in the plain, about 3.5 km west of the Rhine which is also border to Germany. As a part of the Maginot line this bunker was built right outside the village towards Germany.
