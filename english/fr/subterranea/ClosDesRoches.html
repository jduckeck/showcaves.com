History

JUL-1992 restaurant opened.

Description

The restaurant Le Clos des Roches (Vineyeard of Rocks) was the dream of Jean-Paul Locquet, who opened it in 1992. After his death his son and daughter in law operate it. The restaurant is located in 16th century cellars, older than most others along the Loire, smaller and more interesting. A little off the beaten track it is less touristic than other underground restaurants.
