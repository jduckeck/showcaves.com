History


Description

Located at the river road on the northern side of the Loire river, this caverns built into the cliff face were created as underground quarries. Later used for mushroom growing the were also used silkworm-production. Owned by Blanc Foussy vinery it offers wine tasting of the famous white wines after the visit.
