History

16th century built.
1956 opened to the public.

Description

This Caverne Sculptée is exactly what the name says: a cavern of sculptures. The walls and ceiling are covered with fascinating sculptures, grotesque and sometimes obscene. The reason why and by whom this sculptures were created is unknown. The age is also unsure, but it may have been during the 16th century.
