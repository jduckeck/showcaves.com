History

1992 opened to the public.

Description

This is a huge underground quarry where rocks were mined for sculpting, for buildings and for churches. The site was reopened as a tourist site and offers courses to work with rock and and learn a little sculpting and stonecutting. It has an exhibition of masonry tools and of the results, various cut and sculpted works. The rocks from Aubigny were used for various famous builings in Paris, for example the Opéra Garnier, the Hôtel de Ville and the Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers.
