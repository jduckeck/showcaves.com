History

16-OCT-1920 discovered.
16-MAY-1925 classified a Monument Historique (historic monument).

Description

The Grotte Prehistorique des Merveilles contains 20,000 years old (Magdalénien) prehistoric paintings. The paintings show a horse, stags and six outlines of human hands.

The cave was named after its speleothems, which look like local pastries known in the region as merveilles (wonders).
