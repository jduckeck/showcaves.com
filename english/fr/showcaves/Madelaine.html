History

1887 discovered.

Description

Grotte de la Madelaine is the only show cave, located directly at the Ardèche gorge. The cave has a parking lot on top of the plateau, at the Ardèche road. From here a path leads down to the edge of the gorge, and then along the cliff to the entrance of the cave. From this path, the visitor has a very nice view on the Ardèche river, 200 meters below. This is the deepest and most impressive part of the gorge. Even if you do not want to visit the cave, the belvedere in front of the cave is accessible without restrictions and for free, and considered the most beautiful of the gorge. Many people are canoeing down the Ardèche and visit the cave by walking up to the cave entrance.

The cave of the Madeleine is actually made up of two caves: the Lescure Cave and the Madeleine Cave.
