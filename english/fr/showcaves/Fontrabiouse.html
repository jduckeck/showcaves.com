History

1958 discovered during marble quarrying.

Description

Grotte de Fontrabiouse, named after the homonymous village, is a rather young discovery. It was opened during quarry works, developed as a show caves and now draws tourists to this rather remote village. The cave is huge and full of impressive stalactites and stalagmites. The biggest chamber is 25 m high.
