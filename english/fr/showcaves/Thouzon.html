    Description

    Les Grottes de Thouzon (Cave of Thouzon) is the only show cave in the department of Vaucluse. It was named after its location, the hill north of Le Thor, where it is located, is called Thouzon. This cave is famous for its overwhemlming richness in speleothems. There are soda straws, helictites, gours, cave pearls, and draperies. Some soda straws reach a length of two metres, while having a diameter of only three milimetres.
