History

1825 discovered when the road past the entrance was contructed.
1922 developed and opened to the public.

Description

Les Grottes de Presque are famous for their speleothems. Most impressive are pillarlike stalagmites, rather slim and up to eight meters high. The cave is small but impressive because of its various speleothems.
