10-OCT-1981 cave explored.

Description

The Grotte du Clos del Pous is a semi wild cave which is regularly guided as a show cave. The tour is offered by the Office de Tourisme du Laissagais and guided by cavers of the Club Spéléo de la MJC de Rodez. Participants must be at last 10 years old and in good physical fitness. The tour shows the central passages of the cave.

The Grotte du Clos del Pous is the most important cave of the Causse de Séverac. It was discovered in 1981, but has been subject to pollution during the last years and was closed for many years. The problem with the pollution was solved some years ago, and the caving club decided to clean the cave. Attitionaly, the entrance was secured with a concrete well with a diameter of one meter and an iron ladder. There is a gate, which is locked, visitors are asked to contact the tourist infotmation. The entrance is located on private property, trespassing is allowed as long as the grazing cows are not disturbed and the cars are parked correctly.
