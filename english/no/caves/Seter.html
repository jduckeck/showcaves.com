Setergrotta


Useful Information

Location: 22km north of Mo i Rana, Nordland. From Mo i Rana E6 east toward Røssvoll 12km, then 10km into Rødvassdalen, signposted.
Open: 15-JUN to 04-JUL at 15. 05-JUL to 20-AUG at 11:30 and 15. For groups all year by appointment. [2004]

History

 
1947 surveyed by Horn.

Description

Setergrotta is part show cave, as it is closed with regular opening times and fee. But it is not really a developed cave, there is no light or paths. The visitors are furnished with helmets, head lamps, oversuits, gloves and rubber boots. The tours are rather strenuous spelunking tours, recommended only for the physical fit.
