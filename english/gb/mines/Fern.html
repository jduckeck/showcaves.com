Description

Fern and Roman caves are situated on the summit of High Tor. They are a series of very old, worked-out lead veins, and are roofless fissures. No lights are needed. The caves add another point of interest to a pleasant ramble through the personal grounds in which they are situated.


Text from: Tony and Anne Oldham (1972): Discovering Caves - A guide to the Show Caves of Britain. With kind permission by Tony Oldham.
