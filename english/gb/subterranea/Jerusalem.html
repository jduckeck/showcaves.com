History

1189 Ye olde Trip to Jerusalem opened.

Description

Ye olde Trip to Jerusalem is called the oldest pub in Britain, as it claims to have been established in 1189 AD. We do not know if it is really that old, but it is definitely very impressive inside, as about half of the pub is located inside a cavern. But it is not the oldest pub in Britain, this is either the The Old Ferry Boat in St Ives, Cambridgeshire or Ye Olde Fighting Cocks in St Albans.

The name of the pub is obviously related to the crusades. King Richard I is said to have drank at the Trip before embarking on the Crusades.
