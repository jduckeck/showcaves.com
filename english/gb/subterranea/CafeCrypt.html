Description

The self-service café of the church St Martin-in-the-Fields is located underground in the brick-vaulted crypt. It thus called Café in the Crypt. The café is notable for historic gravestones lining the floor and Wednesday jazz. And it is a very good place to have a short break from the bustling city.
