History


Description

Take 5 Bar on Tombland Ln, right opposite the Norwich Cathedral, is an old building with a framework first floor and one of the biggest undercrofts in Norwich. The building is from the 15th century, the rear part was replaced around 1600. The cellar had various tenants during the centuries, a cooper, a china-man, a woolcomber, a gentleman baker and a wine merchant. Today it is the basement of Take 5 bar and used for receptions and concerts.
