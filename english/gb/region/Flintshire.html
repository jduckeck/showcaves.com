Flintshire

Sir y Fflint


Flintshire is a county in north-east Wales and a maritime county bounded to the north by the Dee estuary. The county covers much of the North Wales Coalfield, with the last colliery at Point of Ayr closing in 1996. Despite the major manufacturing industries and mining history there are no underground sites, except for an World War II related bunker system.
