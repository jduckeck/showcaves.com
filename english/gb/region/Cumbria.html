Cumbria


Cumbria in North West England contains the Lake District National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is also part of the Yorkshire Dales National Park, and the North Pennines AONB. Most of Cumbria is mountainous, Scafell Pike (978 m asl) is the highest point in Cumbria and in England. There is no limestone and no caves, but numerous mines and quarries.
