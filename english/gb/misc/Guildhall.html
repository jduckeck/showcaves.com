History


Description

The tours of the Guildhall take place on the days of the City of London Corporation's Court of Common Council meetings. Visitors can attend the meeting after the tour if they wish to. The tour is led by a qualified City of London Guide. Guildhall houses one of the biggest medieval crypts in London which is part of the tour.
