History

1947 maintained by the National Trust.

Description

Lydford Gorge is the gorge of the Lyd river, close to the village Lydford. The narrow gorge has numerous waterfalls and potholes, the most famous are the 30 m high White Lady Waterfall and the Devil’s Cauldron, an impressive series of potholes.
