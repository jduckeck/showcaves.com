History

JUL-1980 Museo Municipal founded.
1997 copper mine closed.
24-JAN-2000 declared a Monumento Nacional.

Description

The town of Minas de Matahambre was named after the local copper mine, which provided work for many people, but was closed in 1997. It was called Mina Capitán Alberto Fernández Montes de Oca. The abandone mine is the reason why the Sitio Histórico de Matahambre (Historical site of Matahambre) is listed as Monumento Nacional (National Monument). There is a small museum, the Museo Municipal, which commemorates the mining days, and of course there are numerous remains of the mining days visible all over the city.
