Cienfuegos Province


Cienfuegos is one of the smallest provinces and almost entirely dedicated to the growing and processing of sugar. At the coast, scuba diving is extremely popular both with tourists and locals, including cave diving. The caves we have listed are located in the limestone hills at the eastern border, most near Topes de Collantes. Those hills are full of caves, only a few of them are accessible for tourists, semi-wild caves which seem to have a sacrifice cave status. We listed those which are officially part of a marked trail and thus visited by hikers on that trail.
