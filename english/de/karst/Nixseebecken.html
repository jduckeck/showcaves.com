Nixseebecken


Location: A7 exit Seesen(Harz), B243 48km through Osterode, Herzberg and Bad Sachsa to Nüxei. At the turnoff to Tettenborn. (29,Ld39)
Klassifikation:  Polje. border Staßfurt Dolomit - Werra Anhydrit
Dimension: Springwiese: AR=2.5ha.
Tal: L=400m, B=50m.
S-Becken: 8Ha.
Fitzmühlenspring: P=20l/s.
