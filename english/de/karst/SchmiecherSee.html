Schmiecher See


Useful Information

Location: South of village Schmiechen. From Ulm B28 16km west to Blaubeuren, then B492 towards Ehingen. Park car at sports area Schmiechen. The lake is 1km to the southeast, accessible by foot of bicycle. (E 9°44'08",N 48°21'00") (9.735555, 48.350000)
Open: No restrictions.
