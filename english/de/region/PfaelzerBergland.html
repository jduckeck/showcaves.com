Pfälzer Bergland


The Nordpfälzer Bergland is a low mountain range landscape in the south of Rhineland-Palatinate and in Saarland. Colloquially, it is usually referred to as the Pfälzer Bergland. As the name suggests, it is part of the Palatinate, but also has a share of Rheinhessen. The highest mountain is the Donnersberg (686 m asl).
