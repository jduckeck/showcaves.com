Geology

The deposit at Johanngeorgenstadt is a hydrothermal polymetallic dyke. The surrounding rocks are mica shists and mica hornfels with a high amount of andalusite and varying grade of metamorphism. There is a range from almost unchanged quartz phyllites to highly metamorphic rocks.
