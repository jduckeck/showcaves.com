Geology


Description

The mining area Freiamt-Sexau was most likely mined as early as Roman and Alemannian times. However, mining is first mentioned in 1234. The start of this mine lost, but the uppermost tunnels, which are obviously the oldest, are thought to originate from the 11th to 13th century.
