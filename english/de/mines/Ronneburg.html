History

1949 first exploration by Sowjet geologists.
1953 start of uranium mining by the SDAG Wismut.
1980 end of mine growth.
1990 uranium mining ended.
1991 begin of reclamation by the WISMUT GmbH.
1997 mines flooded.
2000 Expo on the former open cast.
05-JUN-2004 begin ofreclamation of the waste heaps.
2007 Bundesgartenschau on the former open cast.

Description

In the Bogenbinderhalle the Bergbaumuseum Ronneburg is located, which is dedicated to the history of uranium mining. It is also called Informationszentrum der Stadt Ronneburg (information center of the town Ronneburg). Actually it is an artificial mine which was created in the cellar of the building. It shows machinery and tools from the abandoned mines, mostly mining technology of the 1950s. There is also a small exhibition on minerals and ores.
