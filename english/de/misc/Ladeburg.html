History

 
1982 Begin of construction.
1985 completed.
01-DEC-1986 official start of operation.
1990 taken over by the Bundeswehr.
31-DEC-1991 Bunker abandoned.
2001 purchsed by the Tierheim Ladeburg.
MAY-2002 start of guided tours.

Description

The Bunker Ladeburg is named after the nearby town Ladeburg. It was built as the Gefechtsstand der 41. Fla-Raketenbrigade (command post of the 41st antiaircraft rocket brigade) of the Nationale Volksarme (NVA, east German army) in the 1980s. It was used less than a decade, when the GDR was reunited with West Germany it was shut down.
