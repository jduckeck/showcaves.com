Mittelbau Dora - Mittelwerk Dora


Useful Information

Image: the new built adit is the entrance to the subterranean factory.
Location: A7 exit Hann. Münden/Werratal, F80 east 85km to Nordhausen or A7 exit Seesen(Harz), B243 66km tp Nordhausen. In Nordhausen F4 turn north towards Blankenburg, at the city limits turn left, follow signs to Mahn- und Gedenkstätte Mittelbau Dora.

History

Image: the original entrance B from inside. It was blasted by the Russian army. In the foreground the remains of a V2 rocket motor.
 
1943 alliied air raid on Peenemünde. The V-weapon production Peenemünder Waffenwerk moves into the Kohnstein.
28-AUG-1943 start of the tunnel contruction with the first 107 prisoners.
DEC-1943 first three rockets buitl.
AUG-1944 V1 production in the Kohnstein starts.
11-APR-1945 liberation, first time american soldiers in the factory.
JUN-1945 100 complete "A4" rockets, valuable documents and rocket parts removed by the american soldiers.
1948 entrances of the factory blown by soviet troops.
06-APR-1966 inauguration of the first exhibition in the former crematorium.
APR-1973 new exhibition in four rooms.
1990/91 fisrt exploration of the subterranen facilities.
11-APR-1995 50st anniversary of liberation, inauguration of new facilities.
