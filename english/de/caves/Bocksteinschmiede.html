History

1873 first excavation in the cave Bocksteinhöhle.
1883/84 Excavation durch L. Bürger.
1933/34 Excavation la Robert Wetzel.
1953/56 Excavation ja Robert Wetzel.
2017 inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Description

The Bocksteinschmiede and the Bocksteinhöhle are two small caves located at the northern slope of the Lonetal (Lone valley). Because of their location towards south and the shelter they offered, they were used by the nomadic hunters of the Ice Age during their hunting trips. The caves have been excavated very early, the findings were sensational.
