Brillenhöhle


Useful Information

Location: In Blaubeuren. 16km west of Ulm, exit Blaubeurer Ring, follow the B28. At the city limits of Blaubeuren towards Schelklingen, a trail starts at the right side uphill to the cave. 15 minutes walk.
Open: Closed.
Open on the Tag des offenen Denkmals, Höhlenwandertag.
[2011]
Fee: Free.
