History

Image: typical profile, narrow and high passages leading down very steep.
 
1888first natural caverns mentioned.
1894discovery of more caverns.
1894-1896development as a show cave.
1918first electric light.
1945-1954closed to the public because of safety reasons.
1954restoration of the entrance tunnel and reopening of the show cave.
1968collapse of the ceiling in the area called the Drei Gleichen. Cave closed because of security reasons.
1968-1992closed to the public.
1980after another collapse in the mining part, the Bergsicherung (mining security) did some work on stabilizing the mine.
1991-1992restoration of the cave and reopening of the show cave.
