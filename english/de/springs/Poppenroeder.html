Poppenröder Quelle


Useful Information

Location: Mühlhausen, north of Gotha.
A4 exit Gotha, B247 43 km through Gotha and Bad Langensalza to Mühlhausen.
A7 exit Hannöversch Münden/Werratal, B80 49 km through Witzenhausen to Leinefelde, B247 26 km south to Mühlhausen.
Poppenrode is a western quarter of Mühlhausen, from the B249 turn south into the Quellenweg, follow to the end.
