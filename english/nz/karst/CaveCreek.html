History

 
28-APR-1995 collapse of of a viewing platform at Cave Creek.

Description

Cave Creek Cave is located at the beginning of Cave Creek valley, it is the resurgence of a stream. The water partly sinks at the

  • Bullock Creek Sinks
    • to the north and flows south east underground through the
  • Xanadu Cave
    • system. From the resurgence it turns southwest and flows into the sea.

    Cave Creek is an impressive karst feature, but it became infamous for the Cave Creek disaster. In 1995 a scenic viewing platform above a 40m deep cliff collapsed and 14 people died. The following examination revealed organisational flaws in the National Park system.
