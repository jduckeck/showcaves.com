Description

The Troll Cave is probably the only cave in Waitomo which is not a glowworm cave. The reason is, it is inhabited by Trolls.

According to the Department of Troll Conservation, Cave Trolls are very rare and on the highly endangered list. This the world’s first underground eco-sanctuary for the preservation of Cave Trolls. The Troll living here is named Tom and he is a bit “antisocial”. Go on Troll Patrol and use your stealth, cunning and survival instincts to navigate Tom’s underground lair and complete your mission.

You probably guessed it, Troll Cave is an escape room for kids. Its underground, there is a elevator ride down into the "cave", it seems to be a sort of bunker which was reused.
