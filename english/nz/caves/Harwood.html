Description

Harwood's Hole is a 180m deep daylight shaft into Starlight Cave. The trip through includes this 180m free abseil. Fortunately the cave is a through cave and it is left at another entrance.

The abseil makes this cave rather difficult, and so it is only for experienced cavers or physically fit and adventurous spelunkers. There are some pages about guided cave trips through the cave on the web, but this seems rather impossible. This cave is dangerous, visits should only be made under the guidance of local cavers. The random tourist may visit the rim of the drop but should be very carefull not to fall in.
