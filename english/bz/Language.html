Belize Kriol for Cavers

The language spoken in Belize is Belize Kriol (also Kriol or Belizean Creole), an English-based creole language. Kriol is the lingua franca of Belize and is the first language of Garifunas, Mestizos, Maya, and other ethnic groups, and is the second language for most others in the country. This means there are 150.000 native speakers and more than 200.000 who also speak the language. Nevertheless it should be possible to travel the country with English.
