Description

Jaguar Creek is a 5-star lodge in the middle of the jungle in Belize. Actually, it is a copy of nearby Caves Branch Jungle Lodge, and offers all kinds of jungle and cave adventures. The location is well selected, it actually includes a karst spring, a resurgence from the Cave Branch system, a short river, which then vanishes underground to reappear in nearby Cave Branch river. The spring is located on the grounds of the lodge, so it is freely acessible to guests, but as far as we know not for normal tourists. As they have a restaurant, it is probably sufficient t have a meal there to be a guest.
