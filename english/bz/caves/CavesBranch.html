Description

Caves Branch Jungle Lodge is a privatly owned estate in the jungle of Belize. On the premises numerous caves can be found, which are toured regularly in cave trekking tours. The tours are rather rough end require physical fitness and proper equipment. The cave related tours are River Cave Tubing, Crystal and Mayan Ceremonial Cave Expeditions, Overnight Caving, and Rapelling. Other tours are Jungle Treks, Night Safaris, River Kayaking, Jungle Mountain Biking, and the Expedition to the Lost Mayan Ruins and Tikal.
