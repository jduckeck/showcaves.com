Mtskheta-Mtianeti


The most important sight of this province are found in the Kazbegi National Park in the middle of the Caucasus. It has five important Natural Monuments:

  1. Sakhiznari Cliff
  2. Abano Mineral Lake
  3. Truso Travertine
  4. Jvari Pass Travertine
  5. Keterisi Mineral Vaucluse
