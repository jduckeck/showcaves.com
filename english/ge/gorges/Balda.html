History

2013 designated a Natural Monument.

Description

ბალდის კანიონი (Balda Canyon) is a popular gorge in the upper part of the Abasha river. It is located between the village Second Balda and Balda Monastery of St. Mary’s Assumption, at the end of the road. There is no infrastructure and no trail to walk through the gorge. But it is possible to walk on trails along the gorge some sections are easily accessible. although you might get your shoes wet.
