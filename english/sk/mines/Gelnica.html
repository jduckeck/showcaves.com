Description

The Banícke Múzeum Gelnica (Miner's Museum Gelnica) display documents, pctures, models of mining machinery and even a mine tunnel with ore cart. The collection includes material of the area from the 19th and 20th century. The collection started under Maria Theresia, but the public museum was opened in 1963.
