Description

The Mineralogické a Banícke Múzeum Bojnice (Mining and Mineralogical Museum in Bojnice) is a private exposition of minerals, precious stones and mining created by Jakub Drevennák. The mineral collection conatins 20,000 pieces of minerals, precious stones, and synthetic precious stones and imitations. Exceptional are opals of the Slovak Republic and UV minerals.
