History

14th century first vcellars excavated.
1960s State company Tokaj founded.
1998 demise od the Ste company and vast vineyards, complex technology and a wonderful cellar in Mala Trna (National monument) left unused.
1999 company operates under its current name.

Description

Historická Pivnica Tokaj & Co S.R.O. (Historic Cellar Tokaj & Co, Ltd.) owns the oldest cellar complex in the Slovak part of the Tokaj region. The tufa cellars from the 15th and 16th centuries are a registered National Historic Landmark in Malá Tŕňa. The cellars are vast, and contains 1500 barrels, two tasting rooms and the archive, which actually consits of exceptional wine bottles. There are different tastings, with 2, 9 and 15 samples of wine. All include a tour through the cellars, and the middle an long tasting also include a cold platter. Tastings are held by the head oenologist Ing. Zuzana Čorbová and quite informative.
