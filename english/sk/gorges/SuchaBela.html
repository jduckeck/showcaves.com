Description

Suchá Belá is the most popular and the most famous gorge in the Slovenský raj (Slovak Paradise). Unfortunately it is quite crowded on weekends in Juli and August. One of the major sights in the gorge are the waterfalls Misove vodopády. The gorge is developed with a trails and wooden and iron ladders.

The tour starts in Hrabušice-Podlesok, follow the green hiking trail. While the tour is not difficult, there are some quite exceptional spots. Waterfalls are climbed on ladders which are drilled to the vertical wall beneath the waterfall, elevated trails are bolted to the wall above the rapids. The narrow path made of cellar grate has either no railing or just a rope along the wall. Surefootedness and no fear of heights are necessary for some parts.
