History


Description

Kláštorská Roklina (Monastery Gorge) is rather short but quite steep, an ascent of 220m on only 1,500m. A result are seven waterfalls on the route, which are climbed on ladders attached to the rock beneath the waterfall. To reach the gorge use the cable car at Prielom Hornád where the trail starts. The gorge ends at the Kláštorská (Monastery), hence the name.
