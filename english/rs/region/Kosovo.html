Republic of Kosovo


Kososvo is a partially recognised state named Republic of Kosovo, which was originally part of Yugoslavia, then part of Serbia, and since 2008 independent. However, while 110 UN member states and Taiwan recognized it as a sovereign state, it still has no tld, no ISO 3166 code and no currency, as they simply use Euros although not being part of the EU. As we are ot very much interested in politics - we just sort the website after political borders for convenience reasons - we actualy do not care about the status of the country. So please do not discuss this with us, we are specialized in caves, not in borders. The country is quite small, with only two underground sights listed at the moment, so we decided to keep it as a part of Serbia at the moment, just out of laziness.
