History

1980 discovered.
2007 opened to the public.
2007 declared a natural monument.

Description

Pećina Ravništarka is a horizontal cave with a small cave river called Ponorac. It was named after nearby village Ravnište, which belongs to the basin of Kučajna river, the left tributary of the Pek river. The cave is a very young show cave opened to the public in 2007. On top of the plateau above the cave a visitor center with restaurant and souvenir shop was built. From here visitors descend to the cave entrance below. The huge 12 m wide entrance leads to a single room named Black Castle.
