Тернопільська область

Oblast Ternopil


The southern part of Ternopil Oblast has layers of evaporites, mainly gypsum, which are karstified. There are numerous caves and cave systems of some length. Verteba cave, a cave in Borshchiv Raion, was nominated for the Seven Wonders of Ukraine.
