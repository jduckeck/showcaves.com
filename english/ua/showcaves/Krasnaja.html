Description

Krasnaja pestera is also known under the name Кизил - Коба (Kyzil Koba, Kizil-Koba, Red Cave). It is located below the Chatyr Dagh Plateau, on the road between Simferopol and Alushta, not far from the Emine Bair Khosar Cave and Marble Cave. Part of the system is developed as a show cave, another part is a river cave, which is used for spelunking tours.
