Historic Caves


Historic Caves or Cave Stories? Several famous stories from the Greeks, through the middle ages until today are connected with caves. Even legends often contain a cavern. Caves were always a part of mans life, so they are part of his stories. We will try to collect some stories of this type, and maybe there is a real background or even better: a real cave behind the story.
