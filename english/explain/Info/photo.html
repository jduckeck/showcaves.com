Photography


Taking pictures or videos on show cave or show mine tours is often restricted. If we do not give anything here this means we do not know, it does not mean there are no restrctions. Possible regulations are:

  • Photography/Video allowed
  • Photography/Video forbidden
  • Photography without flash allowed
  • Tripod allowed
