Leander Tell


Biography

 
1895born in Norrköping.
1908learns Esperanto and founds an Esperanto Club in Norrköping.
1966founded the Sveriges Speleologförbund (SSF), the Swedisch Speleologic Society.
1980died in Norrköping.
