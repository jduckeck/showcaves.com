Leander Tell


Biography

1895 born in Norrköping.
1908 learns Esperanto and founds an Esperanto Club in Norrköping.
1966 founded the Sveriges Speleologförbund (SSF), the Swedisch Speleological Society.
1980 died in Norrköping.
