Franz Kraus

28-JUN-1834 - 12-JAN-1897


Biography

28-JUN-1834 born in Wien (Vienna).
1881 systematic exploration and development of the Kraushöhle near Gams.
1887 publishes the first scientific book about speleology.
12-JAN-1897 died.

Bibliography

  • Franz Kraus (1894): Höhlenkunde, Wege und Zweck der Erforschung unterirdischer Räume. Wien: Gerold; 1894
