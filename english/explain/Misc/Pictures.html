Use of Pictures


We get requests for the use of pictures now and then. Its a lot of work to write the same again and again, so here is the simple explanation about the use of pictures from showcaves.com.

  • If the photographer is not Jochen Duckeck, the pictures are copyrighted by the respective photographer and you have to ask him directly. If you send the request to us we will forward it to the photographer, if possible. We will not give you any contact details and do not guarantee that he will answer.
  • If the pictures are by Jochen Duckeck, there are some simple ways to use pictures:
    • If the use is non-profit or educational, you may use the pictures following normal citation rules.
    • If you need full resolution pictures the minimum fee is 80€.
    • If the use is commercial the minimum fee is 80€. This fee applies for any kind of book, magazine, calender, website which is commercial. The use is not exclusive and resticted to the use you tell in your request.
    • All other uses, eg newspapers, titlepages of magazines, use for advertisement, a.s.f., have a higher price and require that you send a short description of the intended use. We have a photographer union in Germany named mfm (Mittelstandsgemeinschaft Foto-Marketing), which publishes a yearly list of typical fees. We normally follow the prices published by them.
  • For any use please respect the following rules:
    • If you want high resolution images, support, additional information you have to pay the fee first. No payment no suport.
    • Payment is by Euro wire transfer or paypal only, and not with direct credit card payment, we accept only account to account transfers.
    • If you pay you will get a German invoice by email with German tax number. There is no tax on the pictures as I am not a professional and do not pay sales tax.
    • We try to respond immediately, but actually there is no guaranteed response time. It may take a few hours or a day to respond, if someone is checking the emails and there may be some weeks in the year when nobody checks the emails (e.g. summer vacation).
