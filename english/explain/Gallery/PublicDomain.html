Public Domain


During the last years numerous websites were created which offer public domain pictures for download. These are pictures where the author releases all rights, so you can use them without any restrictions. There is no need to add a name or a complicated licence tag, no links and no statements. You can copy, modify, distribute and perform the work, even for commercial purposes, all without asking permission. And actually there are many pictures of pretty high quality, high resolution, made by skilled photographers. Here is a list of websites which offer public domain pictures:
