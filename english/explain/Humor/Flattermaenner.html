Nasty Bat Animations

The Horror of Batimation


We made this page 15 years ago, when animated bats flooded the websites. Like any new technology, the www created its own set of bad taste. The animated bats of cave related or otherwise related websites was quite annoying. Fortunately this era is almost over, with the last check we found most links dead or replaced by well done modern websites. So we leave this page as it is, but removed all the dead links.

It is very common on the net, to use images without checking the copyright. Not only images, sound, mp3 and videos, any kind of intellectual property is used without notice. But it is unbelievable how often this strange bat is stolen! Maybe not stolen, many sites owners are sure, that this bat is in the public domain and therefore "free". We found those bats not only on caving pages: biology and environmental pages, Gothic, horror and Halloween pages, even some TV stations use those batimations. They are funny when you see them the first time, but the more you see of them, the more you hate them.... The animation kills your CPU power and faster computers will only show them faster. Still the load is 100%, and your computer is dead. RIP
