Ice and Caves


Ice may contain caves, but caves also may contain ice. Ice is stored in artificial cellars, ice is found naturally in caves. And finally, ice is easily cut, so there are artificial caves inside ice.

With all those possibilities, there are also some restrictions. To create caves in ice there must be a lot of them, and that means automatically glacier.
