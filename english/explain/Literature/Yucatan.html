Cave Hunting in Yucatan

By Henry C. Mercer


The text Cave Hunting in Yucatan by Henry C. Mercer (1856-1930) is actually a speech he held on 10-DEC-1896 for the Society of Arts at the Massachusetts Institute Of Technology in Boston. It was published in 1897 in the Technology Quarterly and Proceedings of the Society of Arts, Volume X.. It was also published as a separate book in the same year.
