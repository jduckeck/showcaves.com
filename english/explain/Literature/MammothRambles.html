Rambles in the Mammoth Cave


The booklet Rambles in the Mammoth Cave was published 1845 as a cave description for cave visitors. It was about 100 pages long and the description of a cave visit by a party one year earlier. It also contained some detailed info on how to get there and six lithographs. We have created an eBook containing the corrected text and the pictures.
