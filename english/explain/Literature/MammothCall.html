Mammoth Cave, Kentucky

Richard Ellsworth Call


Richard Ellsworth Call (1856-1917) was the author of numerous guidebooks into the Mammoth Cave. At some point he published a guidebook together with Horace Carver Hovey, but it seems they later published separarte books.
