Fiction


In the 19th century, a completely new form of literature emerges, the historical novel. Also in this literature form there are references to caves. A special form are the so-called Stone Age novels. Here an attempt was made to convey prehistoric events in the form of a fictional plot. While the literary form of historical novels was invented in England with novels like Ivanhoe, the Stone Age novels are a German invention. The first such novel was by Christian David Friedrich Weinland in 1876 and is called Rulaman.
