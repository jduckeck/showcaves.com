Dobšinská L'adová Jaskyna


The Dobšinská L'adová Jaskyna (Dobsina Ice Cave) is known for a very long time and was described by numerous authors since the 19th century. As those descriptions are now out of copyright we are able to make them available in full length.

  • The Cave of Szelicze, or Szilitze, in Hungary, Dobsina Cave in 1861, by Rev G F Browne.
  • A Visit to Dóbsina Cave in 1895, by Edwin Swift Balch.
  • The Valley of Stracena and the Dobschau Ice-Cavern (Hungary), By Dr. Johann E. Pelech. Translated from the German of Dr. Samuel Klein by W. Bezant Lowe, B.A., F.C.S. Scholar of St. John's College, Cambridge; and Member of the Ungarischen Karpathen-Verein.
