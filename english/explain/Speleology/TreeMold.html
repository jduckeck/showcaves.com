Tree Molds

Lava Casts


A tree is the most common but probably not the only thing, which is meant here. Trees are often covered by a lava flow, and if they are not burnt while inside the lava they decay rather fast afterwards and leave a hole, the negative mold of the former tree. Sometimes they are big enough to crawl through, and thus may be called a cave.

In general any plant, animal or other thing which decays rather small will leave such a cave. There are numerous tree molds over the world, and there is even one cave which is thought to be the mold of a huge rhinoceros.
