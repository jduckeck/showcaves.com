Some Special Cave Types


The following cave types are, despite the name, not actually types of caves. They are aspects of caves, some independent of the formation of the cave itself, or exceptional special sub-forms. They are rather rare, interesting, and above all there are examples that are tourist objects and listed on showcaves.com.
