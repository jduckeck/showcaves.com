Mining Museums


Many mining related museums are located at an abandoned mines, which is operated as a show mine. Typically they are located in one of the abandoned mine buildings. Others are located at town halls, public buildings, or at buildings erected for this purpose. Those museum do not have a real mining tunnel for a true mine experience. Many try to overcome this by adding a sort of mine replica to the exhibition.
