Mining Museums


Many mining related museums are located at an abandoned mines, which is operated as a show mine. Typically, they are located in one of the abandoned mine buildings. Others are located at town halls, public buildings, or at buildings erected for this purpose. Those museums do not have a real mining tunnel for a true mine experience. Many try to overcome this restriction, by adding a mine replica to the exhibition.
