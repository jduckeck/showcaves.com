Museums


Many museums all over the world contain some kind of subterranean display. There are displays of caves and mines. Models of certain parts of the caves are reproduced to show some typical features.

Painted Caves

The most common museums about caves are replicas of painted cave. That's because painted caves are quite fragile. The paintings, drawn by our Stone Age ancestors, survived up to 40,000 years, and are now in danger to be destroyed during a few years of cave tourism. In this case its not something like vandalism, the breathing of the visitors changes the cave air, the heat of the bodies changes the temperature, plant seeds in the clothes of the visitors start to grow in the light of the electric lamps. The result is total destruction in a few years.

Mining Museums

Many mining related museums are located at an abandoned mines, which is operated as a show mine. Typically they are located in one of the abandoned mine buildings. Others are located at town halls, public buildings, or at buildings erected for this purpose. Those museum do not have a real mining tunnel for a true mine experience. Many try to overcome this by add a sort of mine replica to the exhibition.

  • Kimberley Underground Mining Railway
  • Barbarastollen
  • Haus des Bergmanns
  • Oberharzer Bergwerksmuseum
  • Coaltown Museum
  • Owensboro Area Museum of Science and History

Subterranea Museums

Then there are museums dedicated to some artificial underground structure. If the structure itself is not available for any reason, destruction, fragility, or dangers, it is somwetimes repalced by a museum which does the best to reconstruct the original subterranea.

Underground Museums

Finally there are museums which are simply uinderground, probably because the architec found this design cool, or there was not enough place on the surface. Sometime the underground space is available and is simply used for this purpose, without any other rational reason. Or the climate outside is quite bad and underground buildings have some real advantages.
