Museums


Image: a mock up cave shows details on cave sediments in the Höhlenkundliches Museum (Speleologic Museum) in Laichingen, Germany.

Many museums all over the world contain some kind of subterranean display. There are displays of caves and mines. Typically models of certain parts of the caves are reproduced to show some typical features.
