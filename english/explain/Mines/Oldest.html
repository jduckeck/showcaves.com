Oldest Show Mine of the World


It is difficult to determine the oldest show mine, mostly because it’s not easy to say what makes a show mine. We tried our best to collect some very early show mines, but the list is obviously not very accurate.


Date Show Mine Location Explanation
1607 Salzwelten Salzburg Bad Dürrnberg, Austria first guided tours of the mine during the reign of Wolf Dietrich von Raitenau (*1559-✝1617)
1816 Erlebnisbergwerk Berchtesgaden Berchtesgaden, Germany start of guided tours for guests of the Kings of Bavaria.
18?? Salina Cacica Suceava, Romania is a tourist or show mine since the mid 19th century.
1865 Botallack Mine Bottalack, Cornwall, England, Great Britain after the visit by the Prince and Princess of Wales many people visited the mine and the mine operators charged visitors a guinea per person.
