Trogloxenes

Cavernicole - Accidental - Cave Guest


Most animals which are normally known as cave animals are only trogloxenes or subtroglophiles and do not really live in caves. There are actually two subgroups, those who visit caves regularly for certain reasons and those who enter the cave accidentally.


  • Bats
  • Cave Hyena (Crocuta crocuta spelaea)
  • Guacharo (Steatornis caripensis)
  • The Herald (Scoliopteryx libatrix)
  • Cave Lion (Pantera leo spelaea)
  • Swiftlet
  • Cave Bear (Ursus spelaeus)
