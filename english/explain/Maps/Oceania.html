Map of Oceania


Oceania is not a continent, it’s a group of small and tiny islands in the Pacific Ocean.
The islands are quite small and hard to draw on a map, we failed to make one which would work as a clickable map. Only the names of the island groups are clickable.
This is the CIA map which shows more details like underwater mountain ridges.
