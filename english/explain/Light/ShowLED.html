    LED


    LED lamps are rather new, a new technology which developed since 2006 when the first white LED was presented. It took years to develop LEDs which were bright enough and another problem was the necessary voltage, which was not 110V (U.S.A.) or 230V (Europe). Those LEDs needed 5V DC and as a result any light bulb must have a converter.
