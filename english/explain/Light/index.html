Light


Caves are dark, so light is definitely the single most important problem to solve when you want to enter them. Light is such an important topic we started to sort the different aspects into groups, to make them easier to understand.

Light for Cave Exploration

The use of light by speleogists is an important one, and actually a rather young one. Real scientific cave research started only 150 years ago. And since then the lights used for caving were continually developed. While we all depend on scientific and industrial development, the cavers always tended to customize available technology for their needs.
