India


  • Pradip Saligram Meshram (1991): Early Caves Of Maharashtra, A Cultural Study
    vi + 172 pp 19 plates HB DW, South Asia Books, (May 1992), ISBN 8185067732
    Covers historical and geographical background, costume and footwear etc.
    at Amazon.com

  • M S Mate, T V Pathy (1992): Dauatabad: A Reprint,
    106 pp 46 figs [mainly photos] In English SB
    The latest acquisition in a series of books on Indian Cave art.

  • S R Wauchop (1933): The Buddhist Cave Temples Of India,
    reprinted 1981, 121 pp 50 plates, HB DW, Cosmo Publications; (December 31, 1998), ISBN 8170202000
    This book attempts to describe all the caves temples in India, consequently it is a useful reference work.
    at Amazon.com

  • Prabhakar Deo (1993): The Temples Of Marathawada,
    212 pp 69 plates HB DW
    These caves were virtually unknown until recently when Hyderabad was absorbed into the Indian Union.
