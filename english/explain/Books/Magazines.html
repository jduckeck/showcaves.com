Speleo Magazines


Of course, there are caving magazines too. Here is just a small list of them:


  • Spéléo ()
    Le magazine de la spéléologie d'exploration, the "most beautiful caving magazine in the world".
    There is only a French version at the moment, but they're planning an english version in the future.
  • The International Caver ()
    A UK based magazine, not longer available.
  • Descent ()
    Another UK based magazine with an enviable reputation.
  • underground photographer, ()
    The magazine for subterranean images. Specialized on cave photography.
  • Die Höhle ()
    Official publication of the Verband österreichischer Höhlenforscher (Austrian Caving Society).
  • Verband der deutschen Höhlen- und Karstforscher, () The German Caving Society publishes a magazine with 4 issues per year. Scientific articles with English abstracts (). The link leads to the homepage, but there is no separate page for the magazine. Have a look at the List of Publications (pdf file) instead.
  • Der Antiberg, Mitteilungen zur Karst- und Höhlenkunde ()
    German publication of some caving clubs in northern Germany.
  • Laichinger Höhlenfreund ()
    German publication by the Höhlen- und Heimatverein Laichingen, a south German caving club. Published twice a year. Contains typically several scientific articles per issue. English abstracts ().
  • Jaskinie (The Caves) ()
    Polish magazine, very good reputation. They also publish Polish Caving in english ().
  • SPELEOLOGIA ()
    One of several publications of the Società Speleologica Italiana (Italian Caving Society).
  • The Journal of Spelean History ()
    A scientific publication of the American Spelean History Association (ASHA).
  • Der Schlaz ()
    Publication of the Verein für Höhlenkunde München (Munich Caving Club)
  • Journal of Cave and Karst Studies (). The quarterly scientific publication of the National Speleological Society.
  • NSS News (). The monthly publication of the National Speleological Society. Trip reports, events and colour images.
  • Bulletin of the Hellenic Speleological Society () official publication of the Hellenic Speleological Society, the Greek Caving Society.

Additionally there are numerous other magazines which contain articles about caves now and then. Mostly this are geographic magazines.

  • National Geographic Magazine. Numerous famous cave articels during centuries.
