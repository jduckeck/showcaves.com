    Rockhound: An Experience of the North,
    Lulu.com, United States, 2015, Paperback, 168 pp, ISBN 10: 1312992972 / ISBN 13: 9781312992979
    For unknown reasons this book is only available for prices between USD 60 and 80. A realistic price would be a 10th, its just a short booklet about geology and mineral collecting in Canada with 160 pages. Probably interesting if you find it in a library, by not worth this price.

    Castleguard Cave, Canada,

    Types of Canadian Gold Deposits.
    Engineering and Mining Journal, Volume 91, pp 470-472, March 4, 1911. Economic Geology, 6, 247. pdf
    Brief descriptions of the Klondike placer deposits, the replacement lodes of Rossland, B.C. and the saddle reefs of Nova Scotia.

  • A.P. Sabina (2007):
    Rocks & Minerals for the Collector Ottawa to North Bay and Huntsville, Ontario; Gatineau (Hull) to Waltham and Témiscaming, Quebec.
    Geological Survey of Canada Miscellaneous Report 48, 249 pages.
    In the 1960s, Geological Survey of Canada mineralogist Ann Sabina was tasked with developing guidebooks on rock and mineral collecting in Canada to address the growing public interest in geology. The result was an enduring Survey classic of 15 volumes which is for download as pdf from here:
    Rocks and minerals for the collector : Ottawa to North Bay and Huntsville, Ontario; Gatineau (Hull) to Waltham and Témiscaming, Quebec / [by] Ann P. Sabina.
  • László Horváth (2019):
