Germany


We have a very long list of good literature about caving in Germany. Unfortunately there is none, not even one book in English. All books we know of, are in German.

  • Hans Binder, Anke Luz, Hans M. Luz (1993): Schauhöhlen in Deutschland, Taschenbuch, Aegis, Ulm, ISBN: 3870050403
    A collection of the 50+ show caves in Germany. ()
  • Amazon.de


  • Stephan Kempe (Hrsg.) (1982): Höhlen in Deutschland, HB Bildatlas spezial, HB Verlags- und Vertriebsgesellschaft mbH, Hamburg, 1982 ()
