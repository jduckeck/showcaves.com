    1st ed 1938 400 pp 25 illus and two endpaper maps.
    This book describes a journey from the Euphrates to the Nile, and from the Nile to the Tiber. In the course of his journey, Morton visits Babylon, Ur of the Chaldees, The Oasis of Siwa and numerous hermit caves.
