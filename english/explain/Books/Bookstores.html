Speleo Bookstores


Onine bookstore like Amazon.com are always a good place to start, when you look for a certain book. But for caving and mining related books the resuts are rather poor. Unfortunately most caving books are out of print, which is a result of the low number of prints. Publishers do not want thousands of book on stock over years. And bookstores like Amazon have a small stock too, they sell books for the publishers, they do not stock them.

If you are looking for a special book, you may try to find it at a special speleo bookstore. This are bookstores which specialize on a topic, and when books of this topic are published they stock them and sell them over years. This costs money, and so the books are often more expensive. However, you will not be able to buy the book anywhere else because its out of print.

I never shopped there until now. Their website looks like a (very) first generation site and there is no online shopping. There is only a form to print and send by snail mail.

Publications of Speleological Organizations

You will rarely find this publications in a normal bookstore or even in a speleo bookstore. The only way to get them is, to order directly at the organization.
