Armenia


  • Andrzej Wojton (2001): The Caves of Armenia, Jaskinie 3 (24) 2001 Magazine of the Polish Speleological Society [In Polish with English summaries] A4, 34 pp many colour and b & w photos, surveys.
    Two cavers from Walbrzych visited several caves in Armenia. The caves are located between the towns Areni, Agarakadzor, and Gnishik i Khachik. They are formed in limestones and conglomerates.
    Available from Jakub Nowak, ul. Ehrenberga 36a, 31-309 Krakow, Poland.
  • Samvel Shahinyan (2005): Armenian Caves, 172 pp, 198 photos. SB In Armenian.
    The book mainly describes man-made caves which are of great archaeological interest. Mazeli Cave, is a karst cave which is described in some detail, with a survey.
