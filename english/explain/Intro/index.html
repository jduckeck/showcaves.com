Introduction


showcaves.com is a website dedicated to underground tourist sights all over the World. It started as a list of show caves hence the name, and still most subterranean tourist spots are actually show caves or tourist caves. But in some countries there are even more closed or still working mines which offer tourist visits. And then there are other underground sites like cellars, bunkers, tunnels, casemats and so forth. Finally we included geologic site connected with caves, like springs and resurgences, dolines, swallow holes, and even gorges. We list all of them.

The website of the respective site is normally much more detailed than our pages, and most likely more up to date. So our main goals are to offer an overview, what exists, to provide basic information, if possible with open hours and fee, and a short description without marketing adulation. If we recommend a site ts because it is actually interesting. We have no commercial interests so we can truthfully tell you if its great or if its a waste of time.

Every site has one single page. All pages have four parts: facts, history, description, and links. If there are historic texts, further descriptions, ebooks, we offer links for download in the links section. And then we list the best online resources we found during our research.

To find a site on our we recommend the alphabetical index. It allows to search for the name, if you know it. If you plan a holiday we recomend the country list and narrow it down to the area where you want to go. The maps are a nice feature, but extremely incomlete. We do not have exact locations for all sites, and even then we probably have not drawn a map yet.

We have categorized the sites in seven topics, so if you are interested in caves you can ignore mines and subterranea easily.

  • Show Caves and Caves
  • Karst Features
  • Show Mines
  • Springs
  • Gorges
  • Subterranea
