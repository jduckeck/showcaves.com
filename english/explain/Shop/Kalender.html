Speleo Calender


A very good friend of mine, caver for many years, passionate cave photograph and working in a printing shop, made this fabulous calender.

I am very fond, that I am responsible, at least a little bit, that he made this calender. Two years ago I gave him the beautiful Lechuguilla book from Urs Widmer as Christmas present. He was so impressed by the printing quality of this book (remember, he works in a printing shop) that he tried to make something comparable himself.

I am absolutely certain, that the quality of his images and the printing quality are comparable to the Lechuguilla book. But you do not have to believe me, have a look yourself:
