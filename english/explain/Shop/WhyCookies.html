Big Images Not Available Without Cookies


You followed the link to the big version of an image on showcaves.com when you landed on this page.


The reason is easy: you can't view big images without having cookies activated! Please activate cookies in your browser. After you have done this, you will be able to see five big pictures for free.


If you see gray images with an error message instead of the small images, you have cookies deactivated and also an active referrer filter. We are sorry but it is not possible to view any image this way. If you want images, deactivate the referrer filter or reactivate cookies.


Why do I need Cookies? I just wanted to see a bigger version of a photograph....

showcaves.com is on the web since 1993 and it is a free service. But of course it is not free. It actually costs a lot of money for the hosting which is payed by Jochen Duckeck alone. Unfortunately the number of hits and the traffic increases continually which causes increasing costs.

Originally the site contained very little photographs. It is very time consuming and expensive to select good pictures, digitize them and put them online. In the last year the amount of photographs on the website increased substantially which unfortunately also increased the costs for webspace and traffic.

Thats the reason, why we decided to change things a little bit:

  • The original site with informations and pictures is still for free!
  • If you want to see magnified pictures, you must activate cookies, which allows us to lock out search engines and spiders from the pictures.
  • If you are a frequent user of showcaves.com we ask you to pay a nominal fee of EUR 10 for free access to all pictures.

There is also the possibility to buy the complete showcaves.com on CD-ROM including all pictures. And this also saves download costs!
