Mines and Mining

  • Mineralogy Database - Minerals, Locations, Mineral Photos and Data

    An extensive catalogue of minerals, existing since the beginning of the internet in 1993 and a valuable resource about any possible kind of mineral. As minerals are always connected to their localities, this is also a huge list of mines.

  • Mining History Network

    An extensive site with info on mining history. Very detailed bibliographies, databases and link lists.

  • The Mine Explorer Society

    A group of friends that have been exploring abandoned mines since the 1980's, predominately in the small village of Nenthead in Cumbria on Alston moor. Very helpful linklist.
