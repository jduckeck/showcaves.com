History


Description

The Caves Hotel is a luxury designer resort with adult only cottage accommodation. There are ten handcrafted cottages in a garden on the edge of the sea. While the rooms and cottages have nothing to do with caves, other parts of the hotel have. There are natural caves in the cliff which were integrated into the hotel design by Greer-Ann and Bertram Saulter. One is used as dining room.
