Casablanca-Settat


In Arabic الدار البيضاء سطات and in Berber ⵜⵉⴳⵎⵉ ⵜⵓⵎⵍⵉⵍⵜ-ⵙⵟⵟⴰⵜ the region around Casablanca on the Atlantic coast is one of the twelve administrative regions of Morocco. It was formed in September 2015 by merging Grand Casablanca with the provinces of El Jadida and Sidi Bennour in Doukkala-Abda region and the provinces of Benslimane, Berrechid and Settat in Chaouia-Ouardigha region.
