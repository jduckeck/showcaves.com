Description

The grottes de Oualidia (Caves of Oualidia) are artificial caves located in the vertical coast near the village Oualidia. They are not a tourist site and hard to find, but they are guided by local tour guides on day trips. The entrance is located on the plain above the cliff, a narrow tunnel leads down. The caves are rather low and have strange rounded walls. They offer a great view on the sea. The caves are obviously quarries in which the rock was mined.
