Област София-град

Oblast Sofiya-grad - Sofia City Province


Област София-град (Sofia City Province) consists of only 1 municipality – the Sofia Capital Municipality. As the city was built in a wide valley on river sediments, there are actually no underground sites in the province. We listed a museum because of its mining related minerals.


  • Alexander Nevsky Cathedral Underground Crypt
  • Museum of Mineralogy, Petrology and Mineral Resources
  • Serdika Metro Station
  • Archaeological Level of St. Sophia Basilica
