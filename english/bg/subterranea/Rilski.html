Description

The Рилски манастир (Rilski Manastir, Rilski Monastery) is probably the most famous monastery in Bulgaria. The characteristic buildings made of white and dark brown stones, with patterns on the walls and arches, and lots of cupolas, are quite impressive. Its also a popular tourist site with crowds during holiday season. (Cave of St. Ivan Rilski) is not far, and named after the same saint, but almost unknown and rarely visited. Thats actually the way St. Ivan liked it, it was his retreat for praying in solitude, in a small natural cave. Later a chapel was built at the entrance, but the cave is almost in the same state.
