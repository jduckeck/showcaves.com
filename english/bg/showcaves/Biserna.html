APR-2019 opened to the public.
MAY-2019 closed for the protection of bat roost.
SEP-2019 reopened.

Description

Пещера „Бисерна“ (Pearl Cave), also known as Зандана (Zandana) is a sort of on-off show cave. It was developed but never open to the pub. When they finally opened it after years, it was open only for a month and then it was closed again for the protection of a bat roost in the cave. 14 species of protected bats European funding for development Named pearl because of the pearl like drops of water on the stalactites.
