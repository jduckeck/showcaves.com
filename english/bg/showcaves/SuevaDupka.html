History

1963 desclared a natural landmark.

Description

The Sueva Dupka (Soybean Hole) is interesting for different cave species. Scientists found, that primitive man inhabited the cave. A coin with the image of the Roman emperor Antoninus was also found in the cave. During the Ottoman occupation of Bulgaria the cave was used by the locals as a hideout.
