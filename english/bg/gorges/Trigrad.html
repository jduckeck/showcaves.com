Description

The Триградското ждрело (Trigrad Gorge) is located in the Rodope mountains. The narrow gorge is crossed by a road, which becomes pretty narrow because of the restricted space. It is often only a single lane road and has many tunnels. It is also the location of numerous caves, including one show cave. The impressive gorge is a popular tourist destination.
