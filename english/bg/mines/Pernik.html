History

1891 mine opened.
1953 City Museum established.
1957 first exhibition opened.
1959 became the Regional National Museum.
1966 mine cloased.
1968 became the Regional Historical Museum Pernik, administrative and structural changes.
1973 Historical Museum exposition opened.
1978 museum renovated.

Description

The Подземният минен музей (Underground Mining Museum) is located inside two former mine tunnels. In 30 stops the history of coal mining, from manual extraction, over mine carts pulled by horses to the modern mining machinery is presented. The mining museum is part of the Pernik Regional History Museum, which is distributed on several building in the huge park. The tunnels are also used for events on occasion, for example there has been a fashion show a few years ago. But as the space is very limited it depends on the size of the event.
