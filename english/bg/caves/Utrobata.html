Description

Утробата (Utrobata) translates The Womb, and is obviously a reference to the cave entrance which resembles a human vulva. The inside of the cave is said to resembles a uterus, but that’s probably a little exaggerated. It seems that English-speaking people are a little shameful and therefore unable to translate the name because of the sexual innuendo. They call it this cave Utroba Cave. We do not have this problem and use the transliterated original name. The cave is also named Пещера Утроба (Peshtera Utroba, Womb Cave), but the locals actually call it (Blaring Rock)

This is a small cave, and it is not developed, the importance of this cave lies in the archaeological remains which were found here.
