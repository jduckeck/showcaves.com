History


Description

Σπήλαιο της Αγίας Βαρβάρας (Cave of Saint Barbara) is named after the church which is located right below the cave. It is located on the southern slopes of Troutsoulas hill. The cave has two huge chambers, which are connected by a narrow passage. In the cave Early Bronze Age remains were discovered.
