History

1992 excavations started by the Director of Antiquities Dr. A. Sampson.

Description

The Cave of Cyclops is located on the uninhabited island of Youra, northeast of Alonnessos in the Northern Sporades. It was used as a temporal fishing station during the Mesolithic and Neolithic times.
