History


Description

The cave Σπήλαιο Αγίας Άννας (Saint Anna Cave) is located at the Agia Anna Beach, after which it was named. The cave was used to build the Spilia Sea Side Restaurant, in short Spilia Restaurant, inside. The restaurant exists since the early 1990s, and it was lately renovated. The restaurant is famous for its Mediterranean-Mykonian culinary experience. Primarily seafood like oysters, mussels, urchins, crayfish, lobsters, and fresh fish are served straight from the Aegean.
