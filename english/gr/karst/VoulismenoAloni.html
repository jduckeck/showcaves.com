Description

Βουλισμένο Αλώνι (Voulismeno Aloni) is translated sunken floor, which is an accurate description. It is an almost elliptical doline with a diameter between 70m and 90m. As it is located at a hill side, the wall on the uphill side is about 20m high and overhanging, while the downhill side is just a flat hill. It formed by the collapse of a huge cavern below. It is located only 50m from the small road, which is actually the old Heraklion-Rethymnon highway, and there is a trail into the doline. The flat floor is used by locals nad tourists to create patterns with limestone rocks.
