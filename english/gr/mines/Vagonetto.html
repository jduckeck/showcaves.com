History

 
1924 begin of prospecting.
1927 bauxite discovered.
1934 Silver & Baryte Ores Mining Co. S.A founded.
1967 mining moves to Vagonetto.
1998 begin of development as a show mine.
SEP-2003 opened to the public.

Geology


Description

Vagonetto is as far as we know the first and so far only Greek show mine. It was opened by S&B Industrial Minerals S.A., the operator of this bauxite mine, in 2003. It was created in the abandoned Mine 850, which was the first bauxite mine on this location some 50 years ago.
