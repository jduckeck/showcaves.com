Description

SantoCaves are three traditional Cave Houses which were built around 1900. They were renovated and modernized in 2009 with white walls and LED light. The Kallisti Cave House has 45 m², the Spilia Cave House 37 m² and the Aspa Cave House 30 m². All three were originally the home of a family, today they are rent as spacious double rooms.
