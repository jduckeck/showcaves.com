Description

Vouraikos Gorge is a unique sight, it is not only a spectacularly narrow gorge, the Americans call them "slot canyons", it is accessible only by train. The combination historic cog train, steam engines, and spectacular landscape, is quite unique and makes this railway a popular tourist attraction.
