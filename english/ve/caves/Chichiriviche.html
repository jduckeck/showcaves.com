Description

Cueva del Indio de Chichiriviche is a sort of shelter of overhanging cliff which was used to create petroglyphs by the indigenous people. The cave is located in the Parque Nacional Morrocoy and best reached by boat from the city Chichiriviche. There is a wooden pier and a signposted trail to the cave. The cave is the ruin of a karst cave, the walls still show stalactites.
