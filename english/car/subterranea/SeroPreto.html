History

1808 Holy Virgin appeared to Bernadette at Lourdes.
1958 grotto built for the 150th anniversary.

Description

This Lourdes Grotto was built inside a small natural cave, under the guidance of a priest named Erkamp by parishioners. The cave seem to be a tufa cave, or probably a karst cave, but it seems it was never speleologically examined. We classified the grotto as Goodwill, there is a natural cave, which is great, but it does not resemble the original. And the niche with the statue of the virgin is actually quite kitschy.
