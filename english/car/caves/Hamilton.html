History

 
1935 Lucayan artifacts discovered.

Description

Hamilton's Caves (the plural seems to be intended) is one of the largest cave systems in the Bahamas, named after the nearby settlement Hamilton. As far as we understand it is a series of caves close together. The caves are on the private property of Leonard Cartwright, who offers guided tours. He also provides flashlights. It seems he also guides nearby Mortimers Duho Cave.
