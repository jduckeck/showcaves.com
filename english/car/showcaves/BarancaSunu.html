Description

The name Tunnel of Love became common for this cave, because of its spectacular shaped entrance. It looks like a big heart. There are also some speleothems inside which are interpreted as fertility symbols becaus of their shape.

This cave requires some physical fitness to explore, as there are several steep climbs. Helmets and flashlights can be rented at the entrance. The cave is not a tunnel, as its name suggests, but a complex of passages and has two entrances.
