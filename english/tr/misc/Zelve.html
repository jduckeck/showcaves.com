History


Description

Zelve Monastery was carved into the rock in pre-Iconoclastic times

The town Avanos is famous for its colourful earthenware pots. Numerous workshops allow visitors to watch how they are made. The material used for the pottery is the red clay from the Kýzýlýrmak River, the longest river of Turkey.
