Description

Paradise Pansiyon backpacker hostel owned by Mehmet Bozlak. It offers dormitories and double rooms in caves and more dormitories and single rooms in an andjoning building. The prices are really moderate and the owner is friendly and helpfull, knows the area very well and can give advice on sightseeing.
