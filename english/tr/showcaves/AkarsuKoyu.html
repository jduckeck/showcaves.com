    Description

    Akarsu Köyü Mağarası was named after the village Akarsu Köyü, where it is located. It seems the huge entrance room is of great archaeologic interest. At the moment we do not have more info on this site. Descriptions on the web are even more bloated and unsubstatial as usual. It is almost funny, in a sad way: "People say that there are 72 rooms inside of the cave." or "This cave is a really big one."
