    Description

    Insuyu Mağarasın is a river cave, visited by boat. It is an exceptional cave, as it is the second longest cave of the world with millions of stalactites. And the water of the cave river cures diabetes. We suppose there is also a frog which transforms into a prince when kissed, a hidden pirate treasure, the last resting place of the ark of the covenant, and of course the axis of the world goes right through the last chamber.
