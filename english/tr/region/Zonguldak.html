Zonguldak Province


Zonguldak city, the center of Zonguldak Province is also called the Black City, because of its numerous coal mines. Although it is an important harbour at the Black Sea, its coal mines are much more important for its wealth. However, they are also the reason why the city was in the focus of international attention: in a mining accident some years ago numerous miners were trapped and died.
