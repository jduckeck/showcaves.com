Description

Cappadocia Kelebek Hotels are two cave hotels called Kelebek Boutique and Kelebek Pension. Kelebek means butterfly. Kelebek Pension exists for many years now and has a good recommendation. Now a second hotel was opened, which is called boutique hotel. This seems to be the local term for suites with kitchen.

Although we listed this hotel here, it is not really a cave hotel. Kelebek Cave Pension has 16 Ottoman-style rooms, some at least partially in caves. Kelebek Boutique Hotel is located nearby in a traditional Cappadocian stone house lovingly restored but completely without caves. Be aware that the "cave factor" is low in this hotel.
