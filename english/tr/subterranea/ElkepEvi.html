Description

Located near the top of Esbelli, the hill above the town of Ürgüp, it has both, authentic cave rooms and a view over the Cappadokian landscape. Most guest rooms have private terraces and bathtubs.

The hotel also owns the hilltop. Hilltop Garden is used for the daily breakfast and has an view of Cappadocia. The most expensive room is Hilltop Suite with two guest bedrooms and a salon. Some of the newest rooms have private hamams (Turkish bath).

The Elkep Evi cave houses are owned by an American couple from Miami. They are generally a good address for English speaking travellers.
