Description

Yunak Evleri offers both cave rooms and normal rooms. There are six renovated cave houses from the 6th century and a 19th century Greek Mansion. The cave houses with a total of 30 rooms are decorated in warm Ottoman style. All rooms have private patios overlooking the phantastic landscape of Cappadocia.

This is one of the finest cvae hotels and mentioned in many guide books and travel journals.
