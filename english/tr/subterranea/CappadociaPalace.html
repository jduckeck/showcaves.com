History


Description

Cappadocia Palace, also known as Cappadocia Palace Otel (Cappadocia Palace Hotel), is a rather old cave hotel which is located in the city center of Ürgüp. It was transformed into a cave hotel long ago, when the first such hotels were opened, It served as a church in the 6th century, then it was a monastery, but later it was used as cave houses. The hotel has 18 cave rooms, 6 of them have their own jacuzzis. Located in the city center it is strictly speaking right outside the Esbelli Neighbourhood, but as the hotel is virtually the same as the hotels there we decided to list it there.
