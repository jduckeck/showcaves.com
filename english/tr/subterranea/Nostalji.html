History


Description

Nostalji Cave Sui̇t Hotel is one of numerous hotels in the area, with 8 cave rooms, restaurant and free wifi. It was until recently known as Antik Cave House or even Antik Cave House - Special Class, sometimes the name was written as Antique Cave House. We guess the name was too ambiguous and led to misunderstandings, so now it was renamed. There are nice cave rooms which are quite different in style size and price.
