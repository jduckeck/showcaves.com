History

1980 former haylofts, barns and shelters abandoned.
1990 restauration as a hotel.

Description

The Cappadocia Cave Suites is an ensemble of Hittite, Roman and Byzantine houses, equipped with modern comfort. The houses and caves had been used as hayloft, barn and shelter by the locals. A total of 34 rooms, all of which are cosily furnished and equipped with modern amenities, make this a rather big hotel. Facilities include a living room, a kitchenette, a bathroom and a television. Some rooms feature a safe, a minibar and Wi-Fi. There are wheelchair-accessible rooms with accessible bathrooms.

The restauration was made very careful, trying to avoid any damage to the historical buildings. Café Sunset has a terrace offering views of the valley, where you can enjoy drinks and snacks all day long. The hotel is only 300 m from the centre of Göreme with numerous bars, restaurants and shops in the surrounding area.
