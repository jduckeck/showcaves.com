History

1980 former haylofts, barns and shelters abandoned.
1990 restauration as a hotel.

Description

The Cappadocia Cave Suites is a ensemble of Hitite, Roman and Byzantian houses, equipped with modern comfort. The houses and caves had been used as hayloft, barn and shelter by the locals. The restauration was made very carefull, trying to avoid any damage to the historical buildings.
