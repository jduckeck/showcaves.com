History

2006 inaugurated.

Description

The Cappadocia Museum Hotel was named such because it reflects the history of the region by its architecture and the historic exhibits which were used for decoration all over the hotel, including the rooms. Five of the rooms are of the traditional arch type, where a wide but rather low arch first leads into an antechamber, and then a normal door into the room. A bigger sized room with many alcovens cut out of the rock is now used as an art garllery and for concerts.

The hotel is not cheap, but it is newly renovated and has a breathtaking view. And all the rooms are equipped very well, with modern bath, yakuzi, satelite tv and more.
