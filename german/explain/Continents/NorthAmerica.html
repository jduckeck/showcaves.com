North America

North America is a continent which has only three countries, and one of them, Mexico, is only partly on the continent. All three countries have numerous underground sites, and numerous karst areas and geological units. As all three countries are as big as a small continent, the subunit region or state is actually comparable in size to a country, for example in Europe. As most karst areas or mining areas are even smaller they are explained on the state level.
